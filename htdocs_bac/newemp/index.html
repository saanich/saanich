Welcome Message from Paul Murray, Chief Administrative Officer

It takes no time at all to feel welcome in Saanich because we really are pleased you chose us. I remember my first day on the job in 2001. Any anxiety I felt from starting a new job was swept away before the morning break because folks were genuinely friendly. They cared that I would get paid on time, that my family was looked after, that I understood my safety responsibilities, and that I was introduced to my fellow workers. After work, I left the Hall thinking here is a place that practices The Golden Rule: ‘treat others as you wish to be treated’. Several years later, this simple rule still guides Saanich and I hope you have a ‘newbie’ experience similar to mine.

- Paul Murray, Chief Administrative Officer

You've made a great decision to join Saanich!

Welcome to the District of Saanich Team!  We are pleased you decided to join us, and wish you a smooth transition into your new role.  This site is designed to provide you with essential information you will need as you begin your work with us.

Your orientation starts here, with video presentations and information pages that explain our history, future plans, organizational structure, culture, and programs that are designed to support you in your day-to-day life as a Saanich employee.  Your orientation continues at your worksite with briefings with your Supervisor or Manager, support from your fellow team members, and training programs arranged through Human Resources and Safety.  We have also created an orientation checklist for you, to help ensure that you don't miss any importants steps in the orientation process.
