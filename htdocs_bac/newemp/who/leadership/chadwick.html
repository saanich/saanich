Leadership Team

Chief Constable Chadwick joined the Saanich Police Department in 1975 and after completing his recruit training at the British Columbia Police Academy, worked his way through the ranks in various sections of the Uniform and Detective Divisions. In 1996 he was promoted to Inspector and took command of the Uniform Division and in 2001 he was promoted to Deputy Chief Constable whereupon he assumed responsibility for all operational aspects of the Department.  On August 1, 2009 he was promoted to the position of Chief Constable.  As Chief Constable of the Saanich Police Department, he is responsible for the management, guidance, and performance of all activities of the Department and general maintenance of law and order in the Municipality.

Chief Constable Chadwick is Past President of the BC Association of Chiefs of Police as well as having served as the Vice President and Secretary Treasurer. He was  formerly the Chair of the Traffic Safety Committee of the BCACP and serves as a member of the Drug Committee. He also is a member of the Crime Prevention Committee of the Canadian Association of Chiefs of Police and is a member of several provincial working groups and steering committees dealing with road safety, coordination of women’s safety and victim services.

Chief Constable Chadwick is married to Suzanne and together they have 5 children and are the proud grandparents of Grayson, Ella and Audrey.  Born and raised in the Cowichan Valley, and a long time resident of the Saanich Peninsula, Chief Constable Chadwick has coached soccer and baseball and has been active in scouting.  He currently serves on the Board of Directors, Community Living Victoria.
