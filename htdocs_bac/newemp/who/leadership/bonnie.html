Leadership Team

Bonnie Cole started to work at Saanich in March 2001 in the role of HR Advisor, and in 2004 was promoted to the position of Manager of Human Resources. In 2010 Bonnie was promoted to her current position, Director of Corporate Services, a department that encompasses Corporate Projects, Human Resources and Occupational Health and Safety.

Prior to joining Saanich, Bonnie’s posts included Labour Relations Advisor, HR Manager and Director of Labour Relations. She has worked in the Federal and Provincial Governments and at BC Ferries.

Born in Smiths Falls, Ontario, Bonnie is a graduate of Algonquin College. Her volunteer work includes supporting Big Sisters and the Canadian Breast Cancer Foundation.
