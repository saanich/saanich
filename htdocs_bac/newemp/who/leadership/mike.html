Leadership Team

Michael Burgess was born and raised in Victoria BC.  He has served with the Saanich Fire Department for twenty-two years.

After graduating from Mount Douglas Senior Secondary in 1981, he continued working in the meat packing industry until receiving

the call from the Saanich Fire Department.  Chief Burgess started his career with the fire department at age 25, becoming Deputy Fire Chief at the age of 39 in December 2003, and appointed to the position of Fire Chief in May of 2010.  Prior to moving from Operations to Administration, Chief Burgess served in the Department as a front line fire fighter. While a fire fighter he was actively involved with the Saanich Fire Fighters Association serving terms as Union Trustee, Union Secretary/Treasurer, and finally as Union President.

Chief Burgess has pursued his post secondary studies at the University Of Victoria and the Justice Institute of BC.  He holds diplomas in Business Administration and Fire Service Leadership, and is actively pursing a Bachelor Degree in Fire Safety Studies and a Chief Fire Officer Professional designation with the Canadian Association of Fire Chiefs.    

A strong advocate of community involvement, Chief Burgess has been involved with the BC Professional Fire Fighters Burn Fund, has served as the regional advisor for the Muscular Dystrophy Association of Canada, and also as a Saanich Fire Fighter Muscular Dystrophy representative.  He is a founding member of the Saanich Fire Fighter’s Charitable Foundation.
