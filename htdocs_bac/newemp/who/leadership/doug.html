Leadership Team

Doug joined the District of Saanich as Director of Parks and Recreation in February 2010 with a strong and successful executive leadership background in the field. Most recently he was Administrator of the Panorama Recreation Centre and Deputy Director of Esquimalt Parks and Recreation. Prior to those posts he worked in Lacombe, Alberta as Director of Parks and Recreation.

Doug was born in Montreal, and completed his undergraduate studies in Recreation and Business at the University of Waterloo, Ontario. He possesses a Masters Degree in Public Administration from University of Victoria.

An advocate of community service, Doug has served in the role of President for the BC Recreation and Parks Association, he coaches football and hockey, was Venue Chair (Gymnastics) at the 1994 Commonwealth Games and has provided leadership for the Beaver and Cubs programs.
