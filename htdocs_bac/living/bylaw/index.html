The following is a list of some of the most commonly used bylaws and policies that apply within the boundaries of the District of Saanich. The policies provide direction on administrative procedures and help guide future decisions of Council.

The bylaws on this web site are in most cases office consolidations. They are provided for convenience only and should not be used in place of the actual bylaws of which can be obtained from the Legislative Division. To have a bylaw mailed or faxed to you, phone us at 250-475-1775 or e-mail us at clerksec@saanich.ca

We make every effort to ensure that the bylaws on this web site are accurate and contain the latest amendments; however, The Corporation of the District of Saanich cannot guarantee the accuracy of information provided.
