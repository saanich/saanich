In November 2011, the District of Saanich commissioned MXD Development Strategists Ltd. to provide “Population Projections, Trend & Capacity Build-out Analysis”.

The purpose of this study was to provide:

  • Population projections looking more specifically at Saanich and it’s Centres and Villages;
  • Economic sector overview providing insight on potential opportunities;
  • Overview of market supply and demand for retail, office, industrial, residential and other uses; and
  • Snap shot profile of Saanich Centres and Villages providing an outlook on future considerations and related planning implications.

This information gives us a better understanding of major trends that may affect economic development and provides base information for future planning studies. The research also establishes a solid foundation for determining the forecasted growth of land uses within the District of Saanich. 

As the work is quite comprehensive, it has been summarised and presented in two short 10-15 page Executive Summary documents.  The full report is also available below, but may take a few minutes to download.
