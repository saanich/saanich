Child & Youth Inactivity Epidemic

  • 2/3 of Canadian children are not active enough in their daily routines to achieve optimal growth and development
  • 40% of children are less active than 30 years ago
  • Children are progressively fatter, weaker and less flexible
  • Child obesity has tripled over the last 15 years
  • Children on average spend 3-5 hours a day in front of the TV

Benefits of Physical Activity?

  • It is proven through statistical findings that physical activity will increase your life expectancy
  • Physical activity will decrease health care costs nationally up to $2.1 billion.
  • Physical activity reduces the risk of heart disease, strokes and high blood pressure
  • Physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight
  • Physical activity can be used for chronic disease prevention such as Type 2 Diabetes, Osteoporosis, colon cancer and other cancers
  • Physical Activity reduces stress, depression and general unhappiness
  • Functional independence is a great side effect of physical activity!
  • By British Columbians increasing their activity by 10% - our province could save $36 million dollars per year in health related costs
