Posted: November 19th, 2012

Involving the public in discussions on matters that affect them is a long standing practice at the District of Saanich.  The inclusion of diverse opinions, information and experiences has improved the quality of decisions made.

During the period October 19th to November 16th, citizens had an opportunity, through the survey, to tell the district about their preferences in future public participation practices.

The survey results will be posted on the website during the first week of January.  The feedback will be used to guide the development of a public participation policy and framework that will ensure a consistent organizational approach to public participation initiatives.

Thank you for taking the time to complete the survey.
