Parks, Recreation & Culture Master Plan Recommended for Adoption

The District of Saanich has developed a Master Plan to guide the direction of the Parks & Recreation Department over the next 5 – 7 years. The Plan was presented at Council's Committee of the Whole meeting on Monday, July 9, 2012 and was recommended for adoption at a future Council meeting following final amendments.  The next meeting date will be posted here once it has been finalized.
