The survey that followed the June 20th open house is now closed and Elevate Consulting has reviewed all the input collected through the Open House, surveys and stakeholder interviews and have submitted their draft report.

Review of this document is underway and it is hoped that the future direction for the upstairs space in the club house will be confirmed in early September.

If you would like more information or have questions please contact Saanich Parks and Recreation (recreation@saanich.ca, 250 475-5422).
