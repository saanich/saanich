Posted: March 13, 2013

The Distrcit of Saanich and the Canadian Union of Public Employees, Local 2011 has ratified a new four year collective agreement. The term of this agreement is January 1, 2012 to December 31, 2015 and includes a wage increase of 7.25% over the four years.

CUPE Local 2011 represents 1219 regular and auxiliary workers in a variety of positions.

This settlement represents a fair balance between the needs of employees and current economic realities and is consistent with other local government labour agreements.

The District of Saanich services include refuse collection, roads, water and sewer utilities, parks and recreation, environmental protection, planning, building and licencing, and public safety.
