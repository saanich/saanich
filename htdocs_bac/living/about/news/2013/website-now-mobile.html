Posted: March 14, 2013

Visit saanich.ca on any device — no app required. Our new responsive design fits any screen size large or small. The site is now optimized for mobile phones, tablets and desktop computers.

The District of Saanich is committed to responding to the needs of our residents and other recent website improvements include:

  • Calendar of events and advisories show all Saanich events, road closures and building advisories in one place
  • Improved search and navigation including quick access keyword results for top searches and an “I want to...” menu
  • Share our news releases on social media with embedded sharing tools
  • Accessibility improvements meeting Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) standards
