Posted: February 1, 2013

The Canadian penny is being phased out of Canada's coinage system.

More Information

Government of Canada Penny De-circulation

As of Tuesday, February 12th all cash transactions conducted at  Saanich Recreation Centres will be rounded to the nearest nickel ($0.05). Online payments, as well as payments by cheque, debit card and credit card will not be affected. While we will continue to accept pennies as payment, we will not be providing them as change. For any cash payment, only the final amount (or equivalently, the change owed) will be subject to rounding. Individual items, as well as any duties, fees or taxes, will be tabulated in their exact amount prior to rounding. Rounding at other municipal facilities will be implemented in March when software updates have been completed.


For more information on penny de-circulation and transaction regulations, please visit http://www.fin.gc.ca/1cent/index-eng.asp
