Attend a Public Lecture at the David Lam Auditorium, University of Victoria, on Thursday, February 21, 2013 at 7:00 pm

The University of Victoria and the Capital Regional District have collaborated to present a public lecture by Dr. Caroline Andrew, Director, Centre on Governance, University of Ottawa, on “Creating a Welcoming, Diverse and Sustainable Future”.

Dr. Andrew will highlight how we define goals for the places we live and the ways communities design processes to achieve these goals. Reflecting on these processes reveals ethical positions and values that shape the directions taken.

The demographic profile of the Capital Region is changing, affecting our needs for health care, housing, transportation, education and workers over the years to come. This event will assist the community to plan ahead.

Free Admission and Everyone Welcome. Registration and tickets not required.

For more information, visit the UVIC website at www.uvic.ca/newleaf.
