Family Day Activities at Saanich Parks and Recreation

Saanich Events Calendar: Family Day
Families are able to access and enjoy many activities at Saanich Parks and Recreation during British Columbia’s first Family Day Holiday, February 11th, 2013.

Pearkes Recreation Centre
3100 Tillicum Road
250-475-5400
Will be open regular hours from 6:00 am-10 pm and feature the following activities:
8:00-8:50am, Stick N Puck, drop-in
9:15-10:35am, Adult Hockey Skills, program
10:45-12:05pm, Duffer drop-in
12:25-1:45pm, Adult Figure Skate, drop-in
2:45-4:15pm, Everyone Welcome drop-in Skate

 

 

Cedar Hill Golf Course
1400 Derby Road
250-475-7151
Family day golf! After 12 pm children ages 11 – 16 golf for FREE with a paid adult at Cedar Hill Golf Course

Gordon Head Recreation Centre
4100 Lambrick Way
250-475-5577
Will be open from 8 am – 6 pm and will feature an everyone welcome swim with plenty of family oriented games and activities from 1 – 6 pm

Saanich Commonwealth Place
4636 Elk Lake Drive
250-475-7620
Will be open from 10am – 4 pm with everyone welcome swims at 10 am – 12 Noon and 1-4 pm

Cedar Hill Recreation Centre
3220 Cedar Hill Road
250-475-7121
Will be open from 9 am – 4:30 pm and is hosting a Family Day event from 1 – 3 pm with inflatables, balloon animals and face-painting.

Discover Saanich’s Parks, Natural Areas and Trails
Our Parks and Trails are easily accessible, free to use and suitable for the whole family. With 168 Parks and 99 km of trails, Saanich’s extensive Parks system offers a world of outdoor enjoyment suitable for the whole family.
