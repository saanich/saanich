EcoStar Awards - Bowker Creek Blueprint

On September 11, the CRD hosted the 2012 EcoStar Community Environmental Awards, which celebrates environmental leaders across the region – the Bowker Creek Blueprint won in the Integrated Watershed Management category!  The District of Saanich is pleased to be a member of team receiving the award.

Bowker Creek Blueprint - A 100-year action plan to restore the Bowker Creek watershed
