Our Backyard

Our Backyard is a seasonal  newsletter supporting conservation, restoration and education of the natural environment in the Municipality of Saanich.

Naturescape

Naturescape BC is a program that provides educational support to protect, maintain and enhance wildlife habitat.

Native Plant Salvage Program

The Saanich Native Plant Salvage Program is working to preserve native plants in our region and provide volunteers with access to development sites in order to rescue plants.

Annual Environmental Awards

Saanich's Environmental Advisory Committee recognizes people and organizations which made a positive contribution to the natural environment in Saanich each year.

Volunteer Opportunities

Saanich Pulling Together, GORP, and community-led volunteer opportunities are the vital force behind the success of community-based ecological restoration in Saanich.

EcoStar Community Environmental Awards

On September 11 2012, the CRD hosted the 2012 EcoStar Community Environmental Awards, which celebrates environmental leaders across the region.
