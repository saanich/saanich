Jenny Eastman

I coordinate volunteers with the Pulling Together program in Saanich Parks.

With a background in biology and education, this work feels like a natural fit for me. I have managed volunteer programs for parks agencies for more than ten years. I was lucky enough to grow up exploring Saanich Parks and I feel so honoured to work with the many volunteers who are restoring the diverse natural areas in Saanich. I love working with volunteers of all ages and abilities in an outdoor setting.

A highlight for me is seeing volunteers recognize the difference they are making to the ecological health of their parks. When I’m not working with Pulling Together volunteers, I teach biology and math to adult learners, tend my veggie garden and enjoy volunteering and exploring nature with my family.
