The "Climate Change Evolution" brochure is a twenty year snapshot of the key actions Saanich has taken both independently and in cooperation with other municipalities and levels of government to address climate change and promote energy efficiency.

The brochure is a Saanich timeline of events that has helped move Saanich's response to climate change to where it is today.

Sustainable Steps Towards a Greener Saanich

2007 Saanich joins BC Hydro Community Challenge
2007 E-Fleet Program Initiated
2007 CRD Community Energy Plan Completed
2006 Climate Change and Energy Action Plan Initiated
2006 Two Sewer Lift Stations Converted to Gravity
2006 Centennial Trails Phase 1 Completed
2006 Partners for Climate Protection - Milestone 1 Reached
2006 Consumption-based Sewer User Charges Introduced
2006 Sustainability Checklist Applied to new Development
2006 Sustainable Saanich Lecture Series Launched
2006 Private Sector Green Building Policy Developed (CAEE)
2006 Fleet Anti-Idling Program Launched
2006 Bio-diesel Fuel Introduced for Fleet Use
2005 Car Sharing Co-ops and Transit Passes
2005 Pilot Community Energy Efficiency Program (CAEE)
2005 Low Energy Use Asphalt Introduced on Saanich streets
2005 Green Building Policy (LEED Silver for Municipal Bldgs)
