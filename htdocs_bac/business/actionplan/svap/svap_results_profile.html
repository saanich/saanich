Below you will find general information relating to the survey respondents, such as where they live (inside or outside the study area) and their modes of travel through the Valley.

After each question is a pie chart or bar graph showing a summary of all responses to the question.  Not all of the respondents answered every question.  The number of respondents who did answer a specific question is shown in brackets ( ) after each question.

Cross tabulations relevant to each question have also been provided.  For example, a number of questions are cross tabbed according to where the respondent lives: in the Study Area; in Saanich but not the Study Area, and in another municipality.  Other cross tabs are based on the mode of transportation most often used by the respondent when travelling through the Shelbourne Valley: walking, cycling, transit and private motor vehicle.
