Welcome to Careers@Saanich. This page provides some information for you to consider when applying for a position with the District of Saanich.

How Do I Apply?

It is our policy not to accept general applications for employment. Applications for employment are accepted for vacancies that are advertised on our website, our job posting board located at the Municipal Hall, and selectively, in local newspapers and on Civic Info BC. To view current vacancies, click on current employment opportunities.

Applicants are responsible for ensuring that their application form and/or resumé are received by midnight on the closing date and that they clearly indicate how they meet each of the stated requirements. The job description provides the job duties, qualifications and experience required. You can view the job description by clicking on the job title in the vacancy notice.

Applicants are required to submit a separate application form and/or resumé for each competition they wish to be considered for. Ensure that you include a telephone number where you can be contacted. You must also ensure that you include the competition number for the position you are applying for.

An applicant must be a Canadian citizen, landed immigrant or have a legal permit to work in Canada.

We thank all applicants for their interest, however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.

Applying By Email

Email your application and/or resumé by clicking on the "Apply Now" button which is located within the vacancy notice. Attach your application and/or resumé to this email. You will need to complete this step for each vacancy that you wish to apply for. Once successfully submitted, you will receive a confirmation email. Please note that we only accept attachments in MS Word format (.doc) and Acrobat PDF (.pdf).

Applying By Fax

Fax your resumé to Human Resources at (250) 475-5550.

How Can I be Notified of New Postings?

Easy, take advantage of our new RSS (Really Simple Syndication) system. By subscribing to our Saanich Employment Opportunities RSS Feed, you will be notified immediately when new jobs are posted. Go to our RSS page to find out more about RSS and how to subscribe.
