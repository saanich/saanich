To book an inspection please call Inspection Services/Bookings at 250-475-5458 and allow at least 24 hours notice, (or more, particularly in the heavy summer construction months) to accommodate scheduling.

* Please note that Saanich does not do Electrical or Gas Appliance inspections, (for Electrical and Gas call the BC Safety Authority at 250-952-4443).

Building Inspection

Any building inspection WITH a valid Saanich Building Permit. NOTE: these inspections do not include private building inspections on existing structures. If you want a private Building Inspection, prior to purchasing a house look in the yellow pages under Building Inspection Service.

Plumbing Inspection

Any plumbing inspection WITH a valid Saanich Plumbing Permit. NOTE: these inspections do not include private plumbing inspections. If you want a private plumbing inspection, i.e. prior to purchasing a house look in the yellow pages under Building Inspection Service or Plumbing Contractors.

Chimney, Fireplace and/or Woodstove Inspection

Any chimney, fireplace and/or woodstove inspection WITH a valid Saanich Chimney/Fireplace/Woodstove Permit. NOTE: these inspections do not include private inspections. If you want a private inspection, i.e. prior to purchasing a house, look in the yellow pages under Chimney Sweep, look for the CWEI Wood Energy Technical Training Symbol, they will clean your chimney and do a report for insurance purposes.

Public Works Inspection

Private Contractor's works on Municipal property to be inspected? Please allow at least 48 hours notice of preferred time of inspection so that the Inspectors can accommodate.
