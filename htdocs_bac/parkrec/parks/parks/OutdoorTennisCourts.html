Tennis Court Courtesy

PLEASE RESPECT THE FOLLOWING GUIDELINES:

  • Courts used on a first-come, first-served basis with the exception of permitted events and Saanich programs
  • 1/2 hour play or one set when others are waiting
  • Encourage doubles play using proper tennis etiquette
  • Courts provided for racket sports only
  • For other courts please visit www.saanichparks.ca
