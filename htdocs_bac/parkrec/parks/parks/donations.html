Saanich Parks would like to thank you for your interest in participating in our Furniture Donation Program.

When you donate furniture, the Parks Department will:

  1. Provide a new bench, or picnic table, installed on a concrete pad. Locations include new park developments or redevelopment and any 'old' style furniture (painted) that does not have a plaque.
  2. Order and mount a cast bronze plaque with suitable wording.
    Sample Plaque (PDF)

The total cost of the above can be found on our Fees and Charges page. The fee is for a ten year term, and you will receive a tax deductible receipt. Once a location has been confirmed, we require a 50% deposit to initiate the process and the balance is to be provided upon completion.

Thank you for considering this important and meaningful gesture. Your contribution will benefit the community for years to come.

If you require additional information or wish to proceed with the program, please contact the Parks Department or fill out a Park Furniture Donation Application Form (PDF).
