Summer/Fall 2013 Free Walking Programs

Enjoy fresh air, beautiful scenery, a friendly chat and some exercise as we explore many parks and trails throughout the region.

Sunday Hikes
Sundays, 1:00 - 3:30pm with Robert Newell & Penny Stevens

Discover the beauty of the Saanich parks right in your backyard. These FREE guided hikes are open to adults of all ages. No registration necessary just drop in at the appropriate time and meeting place. Although these hikes follow mainly chip trails and pavement, there may be some rough or steep sections, so please wear sturdy and supportive footwear. Carry adequate drinking water.
