Mount View Park 
This park was created by the development of the former Mt View School site at 3814 Carey Road (now referred to as Mount View Heights). It is classified as a neighbourhood park, is .54 ha (1.33 acres) in size, and is located in the Carey Local Area. The new park is proposed to include unstructured open space, a playground and picnic area, a seating plaza with shrub and perennial beds, naturalistic features, trees, and pedestrian circulation links.

Recreational Infrastructure Canada (RInC) Program
Saanich Parks has numerous facilities throughout our park system that meet the recreation/leisure needs within our 166 parks and over 750 Ha of parkland. However, much of the system is aging & in need of refurbishment. The focus of the grant was on our Playgrounds (54), Sport Courts (19), Sports fields (62) & Tennis Courts (35). Our staff conducted condition assessments in the spring of 2009 to identify the priorities for this $3M program.
