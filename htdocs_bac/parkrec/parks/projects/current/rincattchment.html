Playgrounds

The playgrounds listed all required extensive maintenance to keep them safe for children and had surpassed the manufacturers recommended replacement timeline. The playground replacement now all meet or exceed current CSA standards.

Sport Courts

The multi-sport courts are designed to accommodate hockey, basketball or other unstructured hard surface play. Refurbishment of the multi-sport courts include new asphalt for all, backstops and netting were also replaced as required.

Sport Fields

Drainage and irrigation infrastructure problems hampered the use of facilities and the successful turf growth at Lochside and Braefoot parks. Improvements to this infrastructure have improved the playing surface for all particpants.

Tennis Courts

At Lambrick park, the tennis program continued to be used despite the condition of the four courts the complete reconstruction of the four courts has been a tremendous success. Twelve (12) other courts were resurfaced using a tennis court coating providing participants with excellent ball play, safety and traction.
