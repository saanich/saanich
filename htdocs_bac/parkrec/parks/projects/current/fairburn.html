This park was created in 2010 and has been developed in 2 phases.

Phase 1 (2010) of the project involved removal of old infrastructure, installation of sidewalk and a walkway connection, and some tree planting.

The second and final stage was largely completed in 2011.  The park's brand new playground and sport court, together with the mature trees and open space offer something for the whole neighbourhood to enjoy.  A new sign and final grass touch-up in the spring will be the finishing touch for this new park.

If you have any questions regarding the project, please contact Becky Goodall, Park Planner/Designer, Saanich Parks at 475-5522.
