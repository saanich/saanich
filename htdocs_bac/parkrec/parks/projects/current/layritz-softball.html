The softball diamonds at Layritz Park were constructed over 50 years ago.  With no drainage, uneven playing surface and an aging irrigation system, these fields were a priority for renovation. 

After the softball season was over, Parks crews moved in and installed the drainage.  Irrigation and some basic grading is underway through the rest of the summer.  The fields have been seeded and outfield fences are put back up. The fields are ready for play.
