Parks and Trail Guide

For more than a decade, Saanich Parks has been providing park interpretation through the use of illustrative information signs at key locations throughout the parks system. We recognize the value of sharing Saanich’s rich natural and culture history. Providing interpretive and directional signs is an integral part of our park development process that strives to satisfy this need.

Recently, with the Centennial Trails project, a new standard was developed and through this process, Saanich Parks is now providing map signs at key locations along our growing trail system.

Council has placed a priority on expanding the program. The goal is to improve the park and trail experience through the continued installation of interpretive and directional signage.
