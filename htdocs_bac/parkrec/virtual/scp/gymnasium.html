Information on availability available here

7,000 sq ft (97’x 73’x31’h) - Capacity up to 400, depending on use and set up; wheelchair accessible, and can be sectioned into 2 equal parts for half gym use.

It has a hardwood floor, 6 basketball hoops, 3 basketball courts (2 courts can be used simultaneously); 3 badminton courts, and 2 volleyball courts. Other sports include floor hockey, soccer, and dodge ball.

For Sport rentals: an additional fee for insurance may be charged, and wooden bladed sticks (hockey ball) are NOT permitted.

For Sport party rentals: the signing adult must remain in the gymnasium and an additional fee for insurance is applicable.

For Banquets, Receptions, and Dances: a sound system is available, and variable lighting features as well. This is the only room in the facility where alcohol is permitted. A small kitchen servery is attached to the gym, however, there are no cooking facilities available. Additional fees, if required, may be charged for Facility Attendants, Custodial, SOCAN, & insurance.
