The Cedar Hill golf course offers lessons and programs for children of all ages. Whether it's recreational or primary sport, we have something for you. The Junior Program is delivered by CPGA professionals with over 40 years of combined experience. Recognizing that each individual child develops at a different pace, this program allows the student to develop skills that can be used for a lifetime.

We work with ages four, five and six using an age specific program with modified equipment and balls, hitting to velcro targets, and having lots of fun games. Ages 7 to 9, we use shortened golf clubs and real balls on the driving range. At older aged levels we then progress to full clubs and specialized programs for kids 10 to 18. The program is very similar to what they use for junior tennis.

The acquired skills are always delivered in a fun and safe learning environment, with every exercise having a future connection. For more information, contact Craig Rencher 250-475-7151.
