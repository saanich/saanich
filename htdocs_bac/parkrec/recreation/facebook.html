Welcome to the District of Saanich Parks and Recreation Facebook page. We have provided this Facebook page as another method to communicate with and engage our residents and customers about programs and services. Please read the following public use guidelines and municipal commitment statement prior to posting your comments. All articles and content posted are subject to public disclosure.

Public Use Guidelines

While this forum is an open one, it is a family friendly one and we ask that you follow our posting guidelines. We will actively monitor our Facebook page and will remove articles and comments that are not related to Saanich Parks and Recreation programs and services. We will also remove articles and comments that contain any of the following forms of content or links:

  • We do not allow content that includes inappropriate, profane, obscene, or sexually explicit language and/or graphic images.
  • We do not allow content that promotes, fosters, or perpetuates discrimination on the basis of race, colour, ancestry, place of origin, political belief, religion, marital status, family status, physical or mental disability, sex, sexual orientation, or age.
  • We do not allow content that is inflammatory, abusive, hateful, or intended to defame or be harmful to any individual, group, or organization.
  • We do not allow content that may compromise the safety or security of the public or public programs and systems.
  • We do not allow content that encourages illegal activity.
  • We do not allow content that comments on, supports, or opposes political campaigns
  • We do not permit solicitations for money or other types of donations on our facebook fan page from outside agencies, businesses, individuals, organizations or other entities
  • We do not allow content or commentary that promotes, opposes or encourages specific political or religious beliefs, or that supports or opposes a specific political party, person, candidate or religious group, movement or leader
  • Our facebook page is not a commercial site and we do not allow content that solicits or advertises commercial businesses, products, or services

Additionally, Facebook has also established specific content rules and as such all content must not violate the Facebook statement of rights and responsibilities

Copyrighted and other proprietary material should not be posted or submitted in any form unless permission to do so is clearly indicated. In posting his or her own comments or other work, a commenter grants the District of Saanich and anyone viewing the site irrevocable permission to copy, distribute, make derivatives, display or perform the commenter's work publicly and free-of-charge

General

This Facebook page is not intended to replace the District of Saanich website or other forms of communication with the Municipality. Questions, comments, or requests for information that are NOT related to Saanich Parks and Recreation programs and services must be directed to the District of Saanich through our website www.saanich.ca or by contacting us directly at 250-475-1775.Our Facebook Site Commitment

This Facebook page is intended to encourage and build community-based informative dialogue about Saanich Parks and Recreation programs and services.  Reading the comments and feedback you provide will help us to better understand and stay in touch with our community.

Our commitment includes regularly posting topics and information on our programs and services that will be timely and of interest.  Your feedback and responses to our posts is encouraged so that together we can build a shared network that informs and educates each other.  We are also interested in your suggestions or ideas for improving the social media experience for our residents and users.

Our Saanich Parks and Recreation Facebook page is not intended to be a forum where questions about our programs and services are simply posted for us to respond to and we cannot promise that we will always respond to every comment and/or question.  For those posts that we do respond to, we will make every effort to do so within the next business day.

If you have specific questions or require information on District of Saanich Parks and Recreation or our facebook page please communicate with us by e-mailing us at ron.proskow@saanich.ca or by calling us at 250-475-5422.
