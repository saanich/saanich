  1. Customer Service Survey
    A general satisfaction survey for all customers of Saanich Recreation facilities, general visits to our rec centres, drop-in visits, programs and services.
  2. Adult Programs Survey
    Complete the Saanich Recreation Adult Survey if you have been a participant in a Saanich Recreation Adult (16 years+) Class or Program. This applies to ALL adult program participants regardless of the type of program or class. Note: This survey is only for those participants in an instructor led "drop-in" or "pre-registered" Adult age category class or program.
  3. Skating Lessons Survey (registered programs only)
    Please participate in this survey only if you are the parent or guardian of a child that has previously been, or is currently enrolled in a Saanich registered instructr led skating program.
  4. Skating (Drop-in) Survey
    Please participate in this survey if you have participated in any drop-in skating (non-registered) program or activity at Pearkes Recreation Centre, or are the parent or guardian of a child that has participated in any skating drop-in program.
  5. Preschool Survey Please participate in this survey if your child is registered in the full year preschool at Gordon Head, Pearkes or Commonwealth place.
