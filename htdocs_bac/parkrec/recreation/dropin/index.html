**Note about Family and Student Fees

  • At all times, children 7 years and under must be accompanied by, and remain in arm’s reach of an adult, while in the pool.
  • Family rate pays for one or both parents/guardians plus up to four children, whether related or not (or all children in the same family, even if there are more than four). The Family Skating Fee is $14.70. Note: The Family Skating fee includes rentals.
  • To qualify for the student rate a student card or letter from the school registrar confirming registration at an accredited institution must be produced at time of purchase.

PAD: Pre- Authorized Debit Plan

The Recreation Pre-Authorized Debit (PAD) plan is an optional payment plan that provides an opportunity to make equal monthly payments for Saanich Recreation one year Passes and designated registered programs.  The payments are made by authorizing automatic fund transfers from a chequing account at any Canadian financial institution, or monthly installment charges can be applied to a valid credit card.  For further information, please inquire at any Saanich Recreation Reception Desk.

2012 Children's Fitness & Arts Tax Credit Receipt Information

Saanich Parks and Recreation is pleased to provide an official Children's Fitness & Arts Tax Credit receipt, for eligible children's courses from 2012, as required. Children who registered in eligible courses, will have an official receipt available to parents or guardians on request.

Receipts may be obtained from our Reception Staff.
