Cedar Hill

Courts at Cedar Hill Recreation Centre operate under a joint use agreement between the Municipality of Saanich and the Cedar Hill Squash Club, a non-profit society.

Drop-in Fees:

Junior: $5.25
Adult: $10.25
Court fee for two players: $20.50
Guest fee (for players who have a membership at another Club AND are playing with a CH club member): $5.00
Junior Sunday Drop-in program: $3.50  

To book a court call 250-475-7121.

Courts are available during Facility Hours

For complete listing of programs see our online Saanich Active Living Guide. Racquets Page

Booking Privileges

Members of the Cedar Hill Squash Club have two day advance booking rights and access to club ladders, leagues and social events. Non-members can book one day in advance. For more information regarding club membership rates and programs, please contact Phil Green at 250-888-3127 (h), or 250-475-7132 (Cedar Hill Rec Centre) or visit www.chsquash.org.

Private Lessons

Contact the Cedar Hill Squash Club at 250-475-7132 to make arrangement for lesson times and rates.
