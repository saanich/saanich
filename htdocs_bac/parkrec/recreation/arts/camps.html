Summer Arts Camps

Summer Arts Camps Brochure. pdf

At the Arts Centre at Cedar Hill this summer, children and youth explore their creative side as well as the world around them through fun-filled arts, pottery, dance and performing arts camps.

The Art Centre’s supportive environment and state-of-the-art facilities encourage discovery and learning. Inspiring hands-on activities led by experienced arts educators provide children with the creative space to exercise their imagination. While having fun and expressing their creativity, children are also developing self-confidence and making social connections.

Arts are Important

In addition to offering a great experience in a fun and safe environment with experienced art educators, Cedar Hill Arts Camps provide children and youth with a number of tangible life-long benefits, including:

  • developing self-confidence and self-esteem
  • improving awareness and observation skills
  • building creativity and innovation
  • making intellectual and social connections
  • learning to set goals and solve problems using multiple solutions
