Programs

Explore your creative side through classes in a range of media including drawing, painting, collage, multimedia and photography. Our experienced and supportive instructors offer a range of classes and workshops for all ages and levels, tailored to your artistic interests and goals. We also offer fun and creative childrens’ spring and summer camps.

More information on upcoming classes and workshops is available under “Arts- Crafts & Visual” in the Adult, Teen, School Age and Early Childhood sections of the Active Living Guide.


Customized programs for your organization or group:

We work with a number of organizations to offer specialized programs. Dependent upon availability, our studio and instructors are available to develop multi-class or one-time programs to suit your needs and interests. Contact us for more information.
