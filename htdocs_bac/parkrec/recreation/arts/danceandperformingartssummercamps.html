Camp Curtain Call with Kaleidoscope Theatre

Experience the magic and fun of acting, playmaking, musical theatre and movement! Young artists collaborate and develop their confidence as performers. Explore scenes & songs from a variety of plays and musicals, and use new skills to develop an original play. Learn about vocal and physical warm ups for performers, play theatre games, improvise, explore character development, comic timing, and projection! By the end of Camp Curtain Call your young artist will have built confidence and awareness of their ability to tell stories using acting, song and movement in a playful, supportive and team-based environment.
