We will be collecting leaves only.  

Fir/Cedar needles, grass clippings, prunings, garden waste will not be collected. These materials as well as leaves can be dropped off free of charge at Saanich Public Works 1040 McKenzie Ave.

  • Leaves to be adjacent to road in rows or piles (no more than 1 metre away from the road).
  • Leaves  to be clear of catch basins, sidewalks, bike lanes and roadways.
  • Leaves  to be on the street side of open ditches.
  • Access to leaves must be unobstructed (clear of vehicles, obstacles, etc.)

For more information call Public Works: 250-475-5595
