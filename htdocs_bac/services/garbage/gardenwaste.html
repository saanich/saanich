You can drop off accepted types of garden recycling at the Public Works Yard 1040 McKenzie Avenue.

Accepted garden recycling includes grass clippings, non-invasive prunings, plants and branches no larger than 2m in length and 5cm in diameter. We do not accept soil, sod, ashes, rocks, tree root balls, animal waste or wood waste. Maximum vehicle size is 3/4 ton - 1 load per day.

For information on residential garden recycling pickup service, call 250-475-5595. Garden recycling can also be taken to Hartland Landfill.
