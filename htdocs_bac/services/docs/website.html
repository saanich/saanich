New Homepage

The District of Saanich is pleased to launch a new website homepage on Friday, July 27th 2012.  Saanich conducted a brief survey in June 2011, to gather opinions about the current municipal website.  The majority of survey respondents felt that the website was not seen as requiring many improvements, additions or changes.  Only two items were cited by more than 5% of respondents, namely that navigation should be improved and that the site should be made cleaner, clearer and/or less cluttered.

In response to these suggestions we have made some changes.  A simpler navigation design has been incorporated into the new homepage.  We have integrated website standards, best practices, and have tried to make the home page less cluttered.

This is the first of many changes that you will see over time to the Saanich website.  If you have questions or feedback please contact norma.louden@saanich.ca, Corporate Projects Coordinator at 250-475-5494 ext. 3444.

Survey Results

One of our strategic plan initiatives this year is to engage citizens to determine what they want in a Municipal website. In June, 2011 Saanich conducted a brief website survey to gather opinions about the current municipal website, social media preferences and to seek participants for technology focus groups.

The survey was well publicized, through the Saanich News, various websites, Community Associations, Facebook, Twitter and our Youth Council. Uptake was low with 277 individuals completing the questionnaire; however 50 respondents indicated their interest in participating in future technology focus group sessions.

A summary of the findings include:

  • Saanich's website is on par with other government sites.
  • There were very few suggestions for improvement or addition to the website. The most frequent suggestion was to improve navigation.
  • Approximately 40% of the respondents currently use social media - primarily for keeping in touch with family and friends.
  • There does not appear to be a strong demand for applications for mobile devices.

The responses received will be used to help guide our planning for future changes to the website. This survey is one part of a series of initiatives to explore the design of the next municipal web platform. In this regard, work has recently commenced to develop a plan for the Saanich website which includes the use of social media tools.

As a direct follow up to this survey, we may conduct additional focus groups to develop and test ideas for improvements to the existing website and development of the future platform.

Coming soon will be a survey for local business to give feedback on the Saanich website. That survey will be released in the last quarter of 2011.
