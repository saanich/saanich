DRAFT: Glanford Avenue Site Analysis Report

This draft report summarizes the existing conditions of the project area. It also identifies potential issues and opportunities regarding the following: transporation and mobility, stormwater management, urban forest and greenspace and the public realm.

Saanich Complete Streets Concept Design - Design Brief

This report summarizes the concept designs proposed for Cook Street, Tillicum Road and Glanford Avenue. The recommendations were based on a combination of elements, such as the streets' needs as defined in the Site Analysis' Reports as well as comments and discussions throughout the public consultation process.
