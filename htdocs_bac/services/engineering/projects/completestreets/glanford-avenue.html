About

The area of interest for this project is a 2.0km stretch of Glanford Avenue between Vanalman Avenue and Ralph Street within the District of Saanich. This stretch is a major road that provides access to the business parks north of the project and Mckenzie Avenue south of the project. In addition, Glanford Avenue provides access to commercial sites, institutional sites such as Glanford Middle School, several parks and residential properties.

This section of Glanford Avenue is within the Carey Local Area Plan - a plan that will help guide the future of this neighbourhood. Based on this plan, the overall "community vision" in this area is one that utilizes social and environmental criteria for major roads in the neighbourhood. This includes providing safe access to pedestrian and cycling facilities near highways, businesses and schools; preserving and enhancing the passive and active recreation opportunities offered within parks; and maintaining nearby environmental attributes such as Gary Oak meadows and Colquitz River tributaries

The new design of Glanford Avenue will meet the needs of motorists, cyclists and pedestrians of all ages, as well as reducing greenhouse gas emissions and improving storm water quality. The new design will improve safety, be more inviting for all users of the Glanford Avenue corridor and reflect the neighbourhood vision as defined in the Carey Local Area Plan.

Updates

08/28/2013 - Update

The Detailed Design phase of Tillicum Road will commence after the construction of the Cook Street upgrades (mid to late 2014).

10/23/2012 - Glanford Avenue Open House Follow-Up - COMPLETE

With the public open house for the Concept Design of Glanford Avenue complete, please fill out the Online Survey Form by Friday, November 9, 2012. The comments will be collected and will aid in the concept design of Glanford Avenue.

10/12/2012 - Notice of Open House, October 23, 2012 - COMPLETE

The District of Saanich will be hosting a public open house for the Conceptual Design of Glanford Avenue on October 23, 2012 at the McKenzie Elemenary School Gym (4005 Raymond Street North) from 4:00PM to 8:00PM.

We wish to present various complete street concepts at this meeting and gather feedback on each presented.  The comments gathered at this open house will aid in the concept design of Glanford Avenue before the project proceeds to detailed design. We look forward in working with you as we move forward with this project.

 

10/09/2012 - Sounding Board at the Red Barn Market - COMPLETE

With the help of the Red Barn Market, the District of Saanich have installed a sounding board outside of their Glanford Avenue store. As sounding boards are meant to be interactive to the public, this location was chosen in an effort to expose the project to the most people local to Glanford Avenue, onsite.

Please post your comments onto the board with the supplied paper and writing material. The District of Saanich staff will monitor these boards daily and collect the data to help with the conceptual design of Glanford Avenue.
