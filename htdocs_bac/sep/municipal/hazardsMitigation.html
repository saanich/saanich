Earthquakes, tsunamis, severe winter storms, pandemics and other hazards will impact our community, our economy and our daily life. Saanich has hazard specific action plans in place for the following events:

  • Aircraft Crash
  • Atmospheric Hazard
  • Explosion
  • Flood
  • Hazardous Materials
  • Landslides
  • Pandemic
  • Seismic Hazards
  • Structural Collapse
  • Marine Transport Incident
  • Utility Failures
  • Wildland Fires
  • Farmed Animal Emergency
