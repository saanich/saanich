Coat Of Arms

Saanich has a motto in Latin. You can see it on the ribbon below the shield. "Populo Serviendo" means "Serving the People."

Cycle Touring in Saanich

The Saanich Bicycle Touring Route circumnavigates the Municipality of Saanich. Wherever possible, it follows the Galloping Goose or Lochside Trails; elsewhere it uses rural or residential roads.

Zomba Kidz

The Zomba KIDZ Project began in 2006 as part of Saanich’s Centennial celebrations. The project was launched in an effort to look outward and acknowledge our place in the world.
