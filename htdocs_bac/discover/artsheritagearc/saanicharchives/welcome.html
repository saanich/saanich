1911 & 1921 Census Project

Volunteer Opportunity

Saanich Archives is looking for volunteers to help transcribe the 1911 and 1921 census sheets for Saanich. This work can be completed at home on your own computer.  You can work from electronic or hard copy printouts of the census.  Some experience with MS Excel is necessary.  Time commitment is flexible. For more information about this project contact Caroline Duncan at Saanich Archives, Tel:  250-475-1775 ext. 3478, Email:  archives@saanich.ca

 

 

 

 

 

 

Saanich Remembers World War One

Saanich Archives is looking for your help to complete the municipality's World War One Honour Roll and add to our records for 1914-1918. If you have documents, photographs, letters or diaries of Saanich residents who served in the First World War, please contact us by email at archives@saanich.ca or by phone at 250-475-1775 ext. 3478. For more information contact Caroline Duncan at archives@saanich.ca or by phone at 250-475-1775 ext. 3478

Visit the Saanich Remembers World War One webpage

Recent Acquisitions

In addition to the corporate records of the District of Saanich, Saanich Archives accepts donations of photographs, drawings, maps, plans, letters, diaries, and other material that document the history of the municipality.

Cordova Bay Association Newsletters Online

The newsletters of the Cordova Bay Association, The Cordovan and Spindrift, have been digitized and are now available online. Hardcopies are held by Saanich Archives and searchable PDFs are available on the Cordova Bay Association's website.
