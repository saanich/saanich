Chave Family Collection
2012-044

From ca.1915 to the early 1920s William Chave and his son Reginald had a grocery store at the corner of Douglas and Boleskine.  The collection consists of family photos, the grocery, and the Chave family home. Also donated was a souvenir of the opening of the new BC legislature buildings in 1898 and Muriel Chave's copy of the Victoria High School yearbook "The Camosun" 1932-1933.

View the Chave family photos on our website photo search using the keyword 'Chave'.

Ernest Glass fonds

2012-007

Collection of approximately 80 landscape and other photographs taken and printed by amateur photographer Ernest Glass, ca. 1930s to ca. 1950s.  Subjects also include construction of the Island Highway; CJVI transmission towers; ferries and other ships; and the SEDCO 135-F offshore drilling rig.  Mr. Glass was a resident of Saanich and worked for Victoria Machinery Depot.  The images have been scanned and are available to view on our website. Donation
