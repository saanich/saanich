Family Records

Thomas & Maude Hall fonds, 367 photographs, 4.5 cm textual records
The Halls came to Canada from England in 1911. Thomas Hall served with the 103rd Battalion during WWI and upon his return became the principal of George Jay Elementary School and later the Saanich Inspector of Schools. The Halls moved to their cottage Stranton Lodge at 1248 Burnside Road West in the early 1930s and together with local nurseryman and designer Arthur Lahmer developed a showpiece garden amongst the wooded 8.07 acres.
