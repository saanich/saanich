Saanich Strategic Plan Adopted by Council

On December 3, 2012, Council adopted the 2013-2017 Saanich Strategic Plan . This update to the Plan continues to provide an important 'road map' for our municipality, helping us link our short-term actions to our long term strategy of achieving the Saanich Vision from the Official Community Plan .

We believe that the Strategic Plan gives Council and staff a clear roadmap to reach the Saanich Vision. It will help focus our energies on what is most important, ensure that we allocate resources to different priority areas in a balanced way, and provide direction to each employee on how they contribute to the Vision. The Plan offers a framework to satisfy Community Charter requirements by reporting meaningful performance results that clearly indicate to citizens the progress we have made towards achieving the Saanich Vision.

The Strategic Plan integrates the plans, priorities and initiatives from existing strategic documents such as the Saanich General Plan, Local Area Plans, capital plans, and the Parks and Recreation Master Plan, as well as incorporates extensive input from citizens. Feedback through the 2012 Citizen Survey, community associations, focus groups, and businesses (through the 2006 Business Survey) has been considered in development of the plan.

Progress toward the Saanich vision is reported each year. Progress for 2011 is shown in the Annual Progress report.
