Do you need a new 100 mm Sanitary Sewer Connection; a 100 mm Storm Drain Connection; to upgrade your existing water service?

You need to contact one of our employees in Development Services 250-475-5570.  Not only can they give you the costs involved, but if you decide you need a new service, they can arrange for you to come into our office, pay the fee(s) and identify the location of the new service(s).

Once the fee has been paid, work is usually completed within three to four weeks. This time frame does vary on time of year and availability of Municipal Crews.

There are some additional charges that may be applicable with the installation of a new 100 mm Sanitary Sewer connection and/or 100 mm Storm Drain connection. They are:

  1. If Municipal Crews encounter rock when installing the connection(s), you will be charged $80.00 per cubic metre of rock. You will be billed the actual cost at a later date.
  2. If the connection(s) being installed is on a collector road, major road, or arterial highway, an additional $1,000.00 will be charged.
  3. If the connection(s) being installed has a depth greater than 2 m, an additional $100.00 per lineal metre will be charged.

Enquiring about services and costs of a property that has the potential for subdivision or re-development?  Contact one of our employees in Development Services at 250-475-5570.

It is recommended that you come into our office to discuss your enquiries. Please note, that every application is different, therefore, we cannot give a set cost to service a property that has subdivision or re-development potential.

You might want to review the Subdivision Bylaw . This bylaw regulates land, area, shape, dimensions, locations, design, construction, works and services "in order to promote orderly, efficient, economical and aesthetically pleasing development, to preserve the established amenities of the Municipality, and to ensure that subdivisions are suited to the use to which they are intended.". The Subdivision Bylaw is also available from the Planning Department at Saanich Municipal Hall for $5.00 plus HST.

Engineering Specifications are available from Saanich Engineering or Development Services for $25.00 plus HST.
