Saanich works in consultation with B.C. Transit regarding the location of bus stops on new routes.

All inquiries pertaining to bus routes and stops should be directed to B.C. Transit at 250-385-2551. All bus stops in Saanich are given final approval by B.C. Transit.

There are approximately 100 bus shelters within the District of Saanich. All bus shelters containing advertisements are the direct responsibility of Pattison Outdoor, also known as Seaboard Advertising.
