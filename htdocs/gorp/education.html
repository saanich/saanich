Garry Oak Ecosystems of British Columbia: An Educator's Guide

Garry oak ecosystems are rare and distinct; they are found only in southeastern BC. They are, however, complex and dynamic systems that support many life forms, humans among them. Garry Oak Ecosystems of British Columbia is a resource that aims to educate and inspire young people on the values of Garry oak ecosystems as well as promote awareness, stewardship and restoration activities.

Teachers and environmental educators will find themes and activities covering plant and animal identification, habitat needs, seasonal changes, journal writing, mapping, First Nations legends, drama, and conservation techniques.

The Educators Guide is managed by Wild BC. To order, please visit www.wildbc.org
