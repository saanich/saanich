Our Backyard is a seasonal newsletter supporting conservation, restoration and education of the natural environment in the Municipality of Saanich.

Our goals are to:

  • Raise awareness of environmental issues and stewardship initiatives.
  • Gain support in Saanich for conservation, restoration and education initiatives.
  • Provide a network for those working for the environment in Saanich.
  • Provide information on upcoming workshops.

If you would like your organization or project profiled in the newsletter, or have other information to share, we welcome your submissions!  We are also interested in any feedback you have to offer.
