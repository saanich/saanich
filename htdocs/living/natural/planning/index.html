Bylaws & Policies

Bylaws and policies are used to protect the environment in combination with education, leadership, and community outreach.

Natural State Covenants

A Natural State Covenant is a legal agreement that is permanently registered against property.

Streamside Development

The Streamside Development Permit Area was adopted by the District of Saanich in 2006 to comply with the Riparian Areas Regulation of the provincial Fish Protection Act.

Environmental Development Permit Area

The Environmental Development Permit Area was adopted by Saanich Council on March 12, 2012 to protect Environmentally Significant Areas.

Stormwater Management

Stormwater is precipitation that runs off the land due to roofs, driveways, sidewalks, patios, etc. Saanich Council has asked for improvements to stormwater management on private land.

ESA Mapping Initiative

The District of Saanich is expanding its Environmentally Significant Areas (ESA) mapping.
