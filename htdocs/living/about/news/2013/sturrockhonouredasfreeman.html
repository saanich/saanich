HOWARD STURROCK TO BE HONOURED AS FREEMAN OF SAANICH

Mayor Frank Leonard and Council will bestow the honour of Freeman of the
Municipality of Saanich upon Howard Sturrock at a ceremony to be held at Saanich
Municipal Hall, 770 Vernon Avenue on Saturday, February 2,2013 at 10:00AM.

Mr. Sturrock will receive this honour in recognition of his outstanding career in public
service acknowledging a commitment and dedication which has benefited the
municipality and Capital Region. In addition to being the former Mayor of Saanich,
Mr. Sturrock was also a Capital Regional District Board Chair and a board member for
the Municipal Finance Authority and BC Assessment Authority.

Mayor Frank Leonard: "Saanich Council is pleased former Mayor Howard Sturrock has
accepted this recognition. Personally, it was a pleasure to work with Howard Sturrock on
Council and at the Capital Regional District as he set an example of serving the public
with respect and commitment."

As a Freeman, Mr. Sturrock will join a small and elite group of citizens. Throughout
history, municipalities have acknowledged the accomplishments and contributions of
specific citizens by bestowing upon them the highest formal honour, "Freeman", much
like being given the key to the city. The Honour of Freeman of the Municipality of
Saanich has been bestowed upon George Pearkes, Constance Pearkes, Freeman King,
Les Passmore, Dr. Hugh Keenleyside, Bruce Hutchison, Ken Middleton, Hugh Curtis,
John Pendray, Dr. Ron Lou-Poy and Dr. Nancy Turner. This honour has also been
conferred upon the 11 (Victoria) Service Battalion and the 11 (Victoria) Medical
Company.
