The Municipal Council of the District of Saanich will consider the 2012 Draft Annual Report at a Committee of the Whole meeting to be held Monday, June 24, 2013 at 7:30 pm in the Council Chambers, Saanich Municipal Hall, 770 Vernon Avenue.
