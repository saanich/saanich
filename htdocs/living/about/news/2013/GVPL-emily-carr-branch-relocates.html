The District of Saanich has approved an agreement to relocate the Greater Victoria Public Library (GVPL), Emily Carr Branch to Uptown.

The move of the Emily Carr Branch is a top priority for the GVPL.  The Library Board recently considered the opportunity to relocate to Uptown and requested the District of Saanich to provide funding.

A long term lease for space in Uptown has been approved and the new branch is anticipated to open prior to the end of 2013.

The library is looking forward to relocating to Uptown and being co-located with a collection of vibrant retail stores and services.

The GVPL will keep the public informed as plans for the move are finalized.  Library patrons are encouraged to visit GVPL.ca for updates.
