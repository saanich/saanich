2012 Survey Results for the Shelbourne Valley Action Plan Open Houses Now Available

More Information

Shelbourne Valley Action Plan

Virtual Open House

The survey results from the three public open houses held last November in the Shelbourne Valley are now available for viewing. The survey, done at the open houses and on line with an accompanying virtual open house, provides valuable public feedback on the ideas and concepts developed through the Shelbourne Valley Action Plan Transportation Study and the Shelbourne Valley Land Use and Urban Design Study.

The ideas and concepts, and the feedback from the survey, will be used to help draft the Shelbourne Valley Action Plan. Further public consultations will occur once the Plan is drafted.
