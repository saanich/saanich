The Engineering Department is accepting public input on the architectural design concept options for the new Austin Sewage Lift Station and public washroom amenities. The station, located at Austin and Gorge Road, will replace two existing aged stations currently located along Gorge Road. Public input as to which architectural design elements of the building structure may best reflect the neighbourhood and public open space is welcome. Please note the intent is not to chose one option but to comment on preferences or concerns of the concepts presented.

Please submit your feedback to sean.elliott@saanich.ca.
