Media Release

The 3 in-person Open Houses are now complete.

A Virtual Open House and on-line survey (identical to the material shown at the above Open Houses) will remain online until December 17th.
Complete the survey

The purpose of the Open Houses is to present the concepts and ideas from the Shelbourne Valley Action Plan Transportation Study and the Shelbourne Valley Land Use and Urban Design Study for public review and feedback. The concepts and ideas, and the feedback received on them from surveys, will be used to inform the policies and guidelines of the Action Plan.
