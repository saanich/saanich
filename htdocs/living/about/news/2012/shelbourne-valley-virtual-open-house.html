Shelbourne Valley Action Plan

Media Release

Virtual Open House

The purpose of both the on-line & in-person open houses is for the community to review and provide feedback on the ideas and concepts of the Shelbourne Valley Action Plan Transportation, Land Use and Urban Design Studies.  These ideas, concepts and your feedback will be used to prepare the first draft of the Shelbourne Valley Action Plan. The initial draft Plan will be presented for public review and feedback through additional in-person and on-line Open Houses in the New Year.

The virtual open house and survey are identical to what was used as part of the in-person open houses.

The 3 in-person Open Houses are now complete. The virtual open house and survey will remain on-line until December 17th.

If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact the Saanich Planning Department.

Thank you for taking part in this important survey, we have now closed the survey while we analyze the results.
