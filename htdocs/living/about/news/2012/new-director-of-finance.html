Posted: June 28, 2012

Mayor Frank Leonard and Saanich Council are pleased to announce that Valla Tinney has been appointed as the District's Director of Finance effective Tuesday, August 7, 2012. Ms. Tinney is currently Director of Corporate Services with the Town of Sidney. She is a certified general accountant and has 20 years of municipal finance experience in the Capital Region.
