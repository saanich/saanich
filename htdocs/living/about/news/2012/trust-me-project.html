Trust Me Project - Now recruiting! Are you a senior or teen in Saanich? Apply now to be a part of this intergenerational community project bridging teens and seniors in the Gorge Tillicum area.

The Trust Me project is a joint initiative between Saanich Parks & Recreation, Victoria Eldercare Foundation, VIHA and BC Healthy Communities. Its goal is to decrease the perceived barriers between these two age groups and help build more trusting relationships, lessen judgments and in the end provide a safer and friendlier community for all.

Recruitment of teens and seniors has begun. Upon interviewing and selecting the group, the project will start with a series of facilitated dialogues between youth and seniors around the issue of judgement, trust and shared community spaces such as malls, recreation centres, theatres, libraries etc. The dialogue group, through this exploration, will decide on a project that they feel will benefit the larger community and encourage inclusiveness and decrease the barriers between seniors and youth. The experience of working together to implement the shared project will enhance the trust between youth and seniors. The resulting model and the process can be replicated by other communities potentially creating unlimited safer shared spaces.

Application deadline is Oct.12th. Contact Julie Wallace julie.wallace@saanich.ca for more information.
