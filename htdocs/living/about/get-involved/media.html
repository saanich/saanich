    Do your part in reducing our impact on the environment. Saanich Parks & Recreation is minimizing the amount of printed materials we distribute. Let us email you Saanich Parks & Recreation updates. These updates may include electronic versions of the Active Living Guide, schedule updates, and special events notices.
  • Block Watch: Saanich Police
    Crime prevention newsletter from Saanich Police.

    Sustainable Saanich is happy to provide you with monthly updates on all of the great upcoming events and opportunities that will help you take action on climate change. We hope that you enjoy this newsletter and it inspires you to learn, commit and take action to reduce your carbon footprint.
  • Saanich Archives
    Saanich Archives collects and preserves archival material relating to the Municipality of Saanich. We welcome donations of photographs and other records that document the heritage of Saanich.
  • Saanich Emergency Program (SEP)
    News from SEP - a division of the Saanich Fire Department that provides the citizens of Saanich with a response to emergencies, major emergencies and disasters.
  • Our Backyard: Natural Environment
    Our Backyard is a seasonal newsletter supporting conservation, restoration and education of the natural environment in the Municipality of Saanich.
  • GoRestore
    Archived newsletters for the Garry Oak Restoration Project in Saanich.
