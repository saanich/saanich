Are you a Saanich Carbon Champion? Join the many residents who are already taking action on climate change while helping Saanich meet our community greenhouse gas (GHG) reduction target of 33% below 2007 levels by 2020. Simply follow our 1-2-3 step process that will guide you to reducing your carbon footprint while saving you big money at the pump and on increasing energy bills.

Now that you are aware of the commitment Saanich has made to reducing community GHG emissions it is your turn to make a commitment. Just commit to using some form of alternative transportation, improve the energy efficiency of your home, reduce the amount of waste you generate and support locally produced foods. It’s easy, and it will only take a minute or less to fill out! We aren’t asking you to select every possible thing that you want/could do to reduce your carbon footprint; we are leaving it completely up to you to identify which actions are most suitable to achieve your personal pledges.

Please Note: Your commitments will remain confidential.

After completing the Saanich Carbon Champion Pledge form please go to Step 3 and start taking action today!
