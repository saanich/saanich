Are you a Saanich Carbon Champion? Join the many residents who are already taking action on climate change while helping Saanich meet our community greenhouse gas (GHG) reduction target of 33% below 2007 levels by 2020. Simply follow our 1-2-3 step process that will guide you to reducing your carbon footprint while saving you big money at the pump and on increasing energy bills.

Now that the Climate Action Plan has set our community GHG reduction targets, it is up to residents, businesses and municipal staff to help make it happen. The Climate Action Plan outlines the commitments we have made and the proposed actions that we have planned to ensure that we meet our GHG reduction targets. A Draft Climate Change Adaptation Plan has also been developed to help us prepare Saanich for the impacts of climate change. In 2007 our community GHG emission totalled 410,953 tonnes. In Saanich the key sources of emissions are:

  • Transportation (63.5%)
  • Buildings (33.9%)
  • Solid Waste (2.6%)

To help reduce these emissions we are focusing on retrofitting older homes, reducing the amount of waste we generate, and improving bicycling, walking and transit infrastructure in our communities.
