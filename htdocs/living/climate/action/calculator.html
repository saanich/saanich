The District of Saanich has developed an on-line Carbon Fund Calculator that allows Saanich residents to calculate their household carbon footprint and then donate to community based carbon reduction projects through the Saanich Carbon Fund. The user-friendly calculator asks you to list vehicle travel data, air trips, hydro bills, gas and heating oil usage, and then calculates how many tonnes of greenhouse gas (GHG) your household has emitted in a year.
