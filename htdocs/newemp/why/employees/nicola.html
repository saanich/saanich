Employee Testimonials

"I have worked in the field of GIS (Geographic Information Systems) for over 20 years, both in government and private industry.  Before coming to Saanich in 2007, I worked for ESRI, a GIS software vendor, as Professional Services Manager and for Natural Resources Canada as a GIS Analyst.

I have always been passionate about GIS and providing service to people needing answers to spatial questions.  One of the driving forces leading my career change to Saanich was the desire to provide excellence in GIS services to the local community.  Being very operationally driven, I appreciate that our local government environment encourages the practical application of GIS to support its daily business needs.  This is very rewarding to me as our GIS initiatives produce tangible and clear benefits to the organization and residents of Saanich, and also to the larger GIS community.

Saanich is recognized as a leader in GIS.  Some examples of this include our award winning web GIS service; a history of partnering on initiatives with internal departments and local government neighbours; and being a sought after workplace for GIS students through mentoring in the practical application of GIS.  We are supported in our work to promote and integrate GIS throughout and beyond Saanich and we believe very strongly in its value to the organization.

I appreciate that Saanich takes an active role in supporting many charitable causes and they encourage staff well being through onsite fitness classes and activities. 

What do I value most about working at Saanich? I work with a great team where we support each other, give and receive mentoring, and strive for excellence with our GIS initiatives.  I appreciate working with bright and dedicated co-workers where mutual respect and professional work ethics are held to a high standard.   I look forward to the challenges and opportunities we are given to improve and grow our GIS.  I am rewarded knowing that the work we do improves efficiencies across all departments and is highly utilized and valued."
