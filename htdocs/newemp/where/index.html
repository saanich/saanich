Official Community Plan

The Official Community Plan (OCP) is the principal policy document that Council uses to make decisions on matters such as land use, growth management, protection of the environment, transportation and mobility & economic development.

Strategic Plan

This is the 'road map' for our municipality. It helps us link our short term actions to our long term strategy of achieving the Saanich Vision from the Official Community Plan. It contains the primary goals of your department.
