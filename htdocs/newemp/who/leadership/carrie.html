Leadership Team

Carrie began working at Saanich in 1988 in the role of Assistant to the Municipal Clerk. In 1991 she was promoted to Deputy Municipal Clerk and in 1999 was appointed Municipal Clerk. In 2005 she was promoted to Manager of Legislative Services.  Within her roles as Municipal Clerk and Manager of Legislative Services, Carrie was the appointed corporate Head of Information and Privacy, and Chief Election Officer. In 2010, Carrie was promoted to her current position, Director of Legislative Services.

Prior to coming to Saanich, Carrie worked in the private sector, for the Province of BC, and for the District of Central Saanich and the Village of Lake Cowichan.  Her roles in these organizations included administrative, financial, and management.

Carrie has lived in Greater Victoria since 1987.  Born in Duncan, she graduated from Lake Cowichan Secondary School, and then attended Camosun College.  She received her designation as a Certified General Accountant in 1991.

Carrie and her husband enjoy supporting a number of volunteer and community service organizations locally, nationally, and abroad.
