Leadership Team

Sharon Hvozdanski has been a Professional Planner for the last twenty years, working on both long range community planning initiatives and urban design projects within the public, private and non-profit sectors. Sharon holds a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Victoria, and completed her Graduate Studies in Community Planning and Architecture at the University of Manitoba.

Sharon worked on contract with Saanich in the early 1990’s and returned in 2003 to again undertake work as a Planner. Subsequently,  Sharon held the positions of: Supervisor of Strategic Planning; Manager of Special Projects; and she assumed the Director’s role in 2009.

Sharon was both born and raised in the Saanich community. Outside of her role as Director of Planning, she is active in both her professional field and community service.
