Leadership Team

Valla joined the District of Saanich in August 2012. Valla spent 5 years working a local CA  firm prior to commencing her career in municipal finance. Her first local government job was as an Accounting Clerk with the Township of Esquimalt. She progressed within that organization to Deputy Director of Finance, and then joined the senior management team as Director of Corporate Services at the Town of View Royal in 2004 and the Town of Sidney in 2007.

Valla was raised in Saanich, graduating from Mt. Doug High School and obtaining her Business Administration Diploma from Camosun College. Valla earned her Certified General Accountants designation in 1991.

Valla is committed to knowledge sharing and mentorship and has been actively involved in supporting other municipal finance professionals through her participation with the Government Finance Officers Association of BC. She served as president in 2010 and 2011.
