Leadership Team

Paul joined the District of Saanich in 2001.  His 25 year background in municipal leadership includes serving as Director of Finance before becoming the Chief Administrative Officer.

Paul was born on the island of Guernsey, Channel Islands, and graduated from St. Sampson's Secondary School.  He worked his way through a business degree at Simon Fraser University and a graduate degree in public administration at University of Victoria, neither which truly prepared him for the world of local government.

On the volunteer side, Paul has coached soccer for many years and more recently completed a stint as President of the Government Finance Officers Association and the Director of the Local Government Management Association of BC and the Local Government Leadership Academy.

To help with stress, Paul recommends always keeping in mind how lucky we are as Canadians to live our lives in such a wonderful country, compared to many others around the world.  A good walk or bike ride doesn't hurt either.

His guiding pearl of wisdom is, “Never argue with an idiot. They bring you down to their level then beat you with experience.”
