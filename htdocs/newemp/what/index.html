Saanich Overview

Welcome to the District of Saanich. We have a unique corporate culture, which makes the municipality a great workplace.

Saanich Commonwealth Place

A full size gym, steam room, sauna, fitness centre, aerobics/dance studio and 5 multi-purpose rooms, a competition pool, wave pool, dive tank, tot pool, proshop, Café, and the Bruce Hutchison branch of the Greater Victoria Public Library.

Saanich Parks

With over 100 kilometres of trails, 50 playgrounds, 30 tennis courts, 2 lacrosse boxes, 149 parks and miles upon miles of beaches.  Check out this 1 minute video.

Pearkes

Two ice rinks, strength training centre, aerobic & dance studio, rental opportunities for meetings & trade shows, sport courts, weight room & fitness studio, teen activity centre, Saanich Centennial Public Library, and Saanich Neighbourhood Place.

Fire

The Saanich Fire Department's mandate is to protect life and property with the Municipality.  The Department is responsible for the prevention, suppression and investigation of fires.

Gordon Head

3000sq ft weightroom and stretching area, 2 pools, river pool, tot pool, 1400 sq ft Dance/Fitness Studio, skateboard and rollerblading park and multipurpose rooms and auditorium.  We even have solar showers!

Police

The Municipality of Saanich, through its governing Municipal Police Board, is responsible for maintaining an independent police department which is supported by civilians and volunteer Reserve Police Officers.

Cedar Hill Golf Course

The golf course is one of the most popular golf courses in Canada with over 60,000 rounds played per year.  This 18 hole, Par 67, 5000 yard course offers PGA lessons, food and beverage services and event management.

Finance

The Finance Department is responsible for the overall management of the financial affairs for the Municipality.

Corporate Services

Corporate Services is comprised of 3 distinct areas: Corporate Projects, Human Resources, and Occupational Health and Safety.

Legislative Services

Legislative Services Department includes the Archives section, and the Lands, Legal, Legislative, and Risk Management Divisions.  The Department provides a diverse variety of external and internal customer services.

Planning

The Planning Department is comprised of five Divisions: Community Planning, Environmental Services, Special Projects, Subdivision & Inspections/Bylaw Enforcement.

Engineering

Engineering provides guidance, design, analysis and administration of Municipal infrastructure, including roads, water, sewers, drains, street & traffic lights.

Cedar Hill Recreation Centre

Four tennis courts, four squash courts, weight room, dance studio, ceramics study, visual arts studios, multi-purpose auditorium and now housing the Community Arts Council of Greater Victoria Art Galleries and office!

Public Works

The Public Works Division maintains roads, bridges, traffic signals, streetlights, sidewalks, refuse collection, leaf pick up, sanitary sewers and a complex waterworks distribution system.
