Earthquakes

Earthquakes are common in B.C. and more than 1,200 are recorded each year. Most are too small to be felt, but an earthquake capable of causing structural damage is expected to occur about once every ten years.

Tsunamis

Tsunamis are rare, but dangerous events. Scientists and technical experts agree that areas around Saanich are not seriously threatened by tsunamis.

Urban Fires

Fire Safety is an important issue for all of us. Residential fires are responsible for a tragic number of deaths, injuries and millions of dollars in property damage and health care costs annually. Most people will never face a major fire but don't think it will never happen to you!
