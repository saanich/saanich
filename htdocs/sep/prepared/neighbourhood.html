Emergency Program representatives present emergency preparedness workshops in our community, helping residents to be better prepared in the event of a disaster.

Emergency Preparedness Starts With You workshop takes individuals and families through a "step by step" emergency preparedness strategy and is taught by trained staff/volunteer instructors at schools, community centres, and other facilities.

Neighbourhood Emergency Preparedness courses teach neighbourhoods to how to look after themselves after a disaster and work together for an effective emergency response.

School Emergency Planning Program assists Saanich schools with emergency planning and preparedness activities.

Business Emergency Preparedness program presentations help local businesses prepare for and recover from earthquakes and other emergencies.
