BC Building Code Change

On December 20, 2012 the new 2012 BC Building Code comes into effect.

All building and plumbing permit applications received by December 19, 2012 will be processed under the current 2006 BC Building Code.

All building and plumbing permits applied for on or after December 20th will be processed under the new 2012 BC Building Code.

For specific details about the changes to the BC Building Code, please visit the

Province of BC’s website - www.housing.gov.bc.ca/building

 

The BC Building Code 2012 applies to all Building and Plumbing Permit Applications received from December 20, 2012.

Blasting Permit

Application Form

Permission to blast within Saanich.

Requirements:

  • To obtain a Blasting Permit you must fill out a Blasting Permit Application form which can be obtained from Inspection Services.
  • An approved Building Permit or an approved Engineering Servicing Drawing, must be in place prior to applying for a Blasting Permit.
  • A completed "Proof of Insurance" document, completed by your Insurance Agent is required, indicating $2,000,000 liability.
  • A valid Saanich Business Licence.

Contact: Inspection Services 250-475-5457

Fee: $30.00 (subject to change)

Building Moves

Application Form

Permission to move a building in and/or out of Saanich.

Requirements:

  • An Application for Building Permit must be filled out. See Building Permit requirements (they may or may not apply but must be taken into consideration)
  • Tax Collector reviews plans to determine the assessed value is 1.25 times the average assessed value of all dwellings within 200' from boundaries of the building area prior to approval.

Contact: Engineering Department at 250-475-5575, or Building Inspections at 250-475-5457

Fees: (subject to change)

  • To remove a building from a site in Saanich: a fee of $100 plus deposits and other fees as determined when applying.
  • To move a building onto a site in Saanich: this fee is based on the value of the building plus the value of work required, a minimum of $100.

Building Permit - Single Family

Permission to build, including all work related to building in Saanich, such works as enclosing carports, finishing a basement or constructing any new interior or exterior walls.

On April 18, 2005 the Municipality of Saanich implemented a new Single Family Building Permit Review process.

A processed permit is defined as either permit ready for issue or a comprehensive letter outlining all conditions or corrections required to secure an issued permit.

Download our application packages below:

  • For the Construction of a New Single Family Dwelling, Duplex or Accessory Building
  • For an Addition to a Single Family Dwelling, Duplex or Accessory Building or Covered Deck
  • For the Renovation of a Single Family Dwelling, Duplex or Accessory Building
  • To add a Deck to a Single Family Dwelling, Duplex or Accessory Building
  • For the Demolition of a Single Family Dwelling, Duplex or Accessory Building

Only complete applications will be accepted for processing. A complete application will include:

  1. Five (5) complete sets of plans (three (3) for a demolition permit) - See page two of application for detailed plan requirements
  2. Completed application form
  3. Current certificate of title (dated within 30 days) plus any copies of any encumbrances registered against the parcel (not required for interior renovation permits)
  4. Owner signature or Authorization of Owner if Agent is making application
    • If not the owner, you need a letter of authorization from the property owner.
  5. Application fees

Building Permit - Commercial, Industrial and Multi-Residential

Application Form

Permission to build, including all work related to building in Saanich, such works as enclosing carports, finishing a basement or constructing any new interior or exterior walls.

Requirements:

  • Zoning must be in place prior to application, contact the Planning Department at 250-475-5473 or 250-475-5471. (Zoning requirement information includes: permitted use, setbacks, maximum building and floor areas, maximum building height, and maximum lot coverage.
  • Building permits for commercial, industrial, or multi-family development will require an approved development permit.
  • If a commercial, industrial or multi-family development is deemed exempt from a development permit as outlined in the Exemptions criteria in the document Development Permit Areas: Justification and Guidelines, but the building permit value would exceed $100,000, these developments will be required to be considered by the Design Panel.
  • An Application for Building Permit must be filled in and a non-refundable deposit paid (see Fee section).
  • If not the owner, you need a letter of authorization from the property owner.
  • If a Contractor is doing the works, they must have a valid business licence.
  • Plans:
    • 5 sets of drawings for new commercial
    • 5 sets of plans for leasehold/tenant improvements
    • all containing: Site Plan, All Floor Plans, All Building elevations and Building Sections/Details
    • All plan-sets are to be submitted in the following set order: Architectural, Structural, Mechanical, Plumbing, Fire Suppressions, Electrical, other
  • All approved services installed (Contact the Engineering Department at 250-475-5575 to confirm if drawings indicating location of Municipal services are available.
  • For septic fields and potable water supply information, contact VIHA at 250-519-3401.
  • A Tree Permit may be required, contact the Parks Dept. at 250-475-5494.
  • Subdivisions must be registered at B.C. Land Registry before the application can be accepted for processing.
  • A Geotechnical Engineer's report may be required, (depending on site conditions). If so, Letters of Assurance must be submitted as well.
  • When a registered professional provides Letters of Assurance, he or she shall obtain and keep in force professional liability insurance and shall provide proof of such insurance to the building official through the Declaration of Insurance Coverage form.

Contact: Inspection Services 250-475-5457

Fees: (subject to change)
Fees charges are based on the value of construction, as determined by the Manager of Inspections Services:

  • For permits $1,000 or less in value: $50
  • For permits greater than $1,000: $50 for the first thousand plus $13 per thousand or part for the next $499,000, plus $10 per thousand or part for all the value greater than $500,000.
  • For extension: $50
  • Non-refundable Application Fee: for construction value of $6,000 or less: $30. For construction value greater than $6,001: $100.00

Deposit of Fill or Soil Removal

Application Form

Permission to deposit or remove fill within Saanich.

Requirements:

  • An Application for Deposit of Fill or Soil Removal Permit is to be filled out.
  • If not the owner, you need a letter of authorization from the homeowner.
  • If a Contractor is doing the works, they must have a valid business licence.
  • A site plan showing the area where the fill is going to be deposited or soil is to be removed (including all natural water courses).
  • A drawing indicating the profile of existing and proposed grades and cross sections must be supplied.

Contact: Development Services 250-475-5570.

Fees: (subject to change)

  • Permit fee is $100
  • Plus a calculated Surety Deposit (refundable): $500 for first 50,000 cubic metres or less for each one-half hectare or part of. If the deposit of fill is over 50,000 cubic metres, the amount is calculated at $1,500 per each one-half hectare or part of.

Driveway Access

Application Form

Permission to construct an access over Municipal property to homeowners property within Saanich.

Requirements:

  • An Application for Driveway Access must be filled out.
  • If your are not the owner, a letter of authorization from the homeowner is required.
  • If a Contractor is doing the work, they must have a valid business licence.
  • A copy of sketch indicating proposed driveway location, complete with water meter, trees, fire hydrants, and power pole locations must be supplied.

Contact: Development Services 250-475-5570.

Fees: (subject to change)

No permit fee, but a refundable Calculated Surety Deposit (refundable) is required, the amount is calculated on the type of crossing required.

Swimming Pool

Application Form

Permission to construct/install a swimming pool within Saanich.

Requirements:

  • An Application for Building Permit must be filled out.
  • If not the owner, you need a letter of authorization from the homeowner.
  • If a Contractor is doing the works, they must have a valid business licence.
  • A plan must be supplied showing location of pool and all water supply piping, waste piping and all related fittings, and the mandatory fence enclosure dimensions.
  • A separate permit for a swimming pool fence must be obtained.

Contact: Inspection Services 250-475-5457

Fees: (subject to change)

  • Separate Swimming Pool Fence Permit Fee: $15.00
  • Fees are calculated depending on application, see Building Permit fees above.
