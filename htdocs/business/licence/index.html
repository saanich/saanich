Business Licences

The Business Licence Bylaw requires that all businesses operating in Saanich be in possession of a valid Saanich Business Licence or a valid Intermunicipal Business Licence.

Home Based Business

Home Based Businesses are those businesses which may be operated from a person’s residence.

OneStop Business Registry

OneStop Business Registry is a computer program that leads users through several application processes at one sitting and then sends the results to the appropriate agencies.
