Council's Office

Through the Council’s Office project under the Emerging Technology Program, Saanich is piloting the delivery of new technology into areas of the organization where it can lead to productivity gains beyond the pure cost of the devices.

iPads are provided to Council and the senior staff in order to evaluate a possibility to cut costs, increase work productivity, and reduce the environmental impact, an important part of Saanich’s overall economic and environmental goals. More on the Emerging Technology Program at Saanich.
