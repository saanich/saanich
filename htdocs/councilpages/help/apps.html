Work with Office documents

Viewing and sharing documents is easy with the built-in Mail app on iPad. No additional apps required - just tap on the file icon in the email.

Ever need to make an urgent change to a document or correct an error after you’ve left the office? Edit or create beautifully formatted documents using the Pages ($9.99) word processing app on iPad. You can easily edit documents on the go, and then share your updated documents immediately via email.

With the innovative Numbers ($9.99) app, you can review, edit, and create Excel or Numbers spreadsheets right on your iPad. Numbers includes over 250 easy-to-use functions, an intelligent keyboard, flexible tables, and eye-catching charts, so you can create compelling spreadsheets with just your finger.

Use Keynote ($9.99) on iPad to easily create and customize presentations—even at the last minute. When you’re finished, export or email your edited slides as a Keynote, PowerPoint, or PDF file.

Use Notes to jot down thoughts, to-do items, and meeting notes. Save your notes on iPad, email to others.

Evernote (free) allows you to capture notes and ideas, add photos and voice recordings, and even tag your notes with geolocation information and keywords.

PDF Reader Pro ($5.99) is a comprehensive business helper that enables you to fill forms, sign documents, highlight text, add comments to PDFs, and access various files wherever you are.l

Need to mark up a PDF? Apps like GoodReader for iPad ($4.99) and iAnnotate PDF ($9.99) let you annotate and present PDF files anywhere.

sChoose GoodReader for iPad if you need to view and manage file types other than PDFs, such as Microsoft Office documents or videos. Use iAnnotate PDF if you need more advanced annotation tools. When you’ve marked up a document with iAnnotate PDF and want to email it to a colleague, choose “Mail with Summary” to automatically add a summary of the comments you made in the document.

Agendas allows you to create organized, networked, interactive agendas. You can broadcast the agenda to other iPads on the same Wi-Fi network, making meetings productive, timely and enjoyable. You can email a PDF version to any email address or use AirPrint when you still need a copy for someone.

Your office away from the office

Video calls in a tap

FaceTime on iPad 2 lets you make video calls via WiFi with just a tap from your iPad 2 to a coworker’s iPad 2, iPhone 4, new iPod touch, or Mac running FaceTime. Use FaceTime to attend or host online meetings with clients, share agendas, and make video calls with colleagues.

Financial charts and graphs

iPad allows you to read reports and analyze data without squinting or constantly scrolling, and as your finger touches the display it instantly transforms your taps, swipes, pinches, and flicks into lifelike actions. Flick through financial charts and graphs. Zoom into specific data sets. Tap to organize key performance indicators.
