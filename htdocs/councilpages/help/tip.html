Assuring your iPad’s security

If you lose your iPad, the FindMyiPad application can help you locate your iPad on a map, display a message, or remotely delete your data.

To enable FindMyiPad:

  1. You need to have a separate Apple ID for this particular device. If you need to create one, see instructions “How to create Apple ID” below
  2. Then, on your iPad go to Settings > iCloud >sign in with your Apple ID
  3. You will have to provide your credit card number to download FindMyiPad and any other free apps in the future (there is no charge, but your Apple ID has to be linked to a credit card number)
  4. Make sure that FindMyiPad is “ON”

How to create Apple ID

Apple ID is a free-of-charge authentication system that Apple Inc. introduced for many of its products. Apple ID serves as an all-in-one account that allows users access to a variety of Apple’s resources (shop the iTunes Store, log in to iChat, buy from the Apple Online Store).

An Apple ID is composed of two parts: username (your email) and password.

  1. Apple ID is available and can be obtained by signing up at My Apple ID webpage:

    appleid.apple.com

  2. To further set up your Apple ID see: apple.com/icloud/setup/

If you wish to make a purchase from iTunes or Apple Store you will have to provide your credit card number with your Apple ID account.
