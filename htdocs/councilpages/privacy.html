Privacy

The Council Info Site has been developed for Saanich Council for the purpose of delivering correspondence and communications in electronic format.

The District is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of the personal information contained in the communications we receive. This webpage has been designed with access securities to protect and safeguard the personal information in our custody or under our control. You can contribute to maintaining the privacy of others by not disclosing the Site Address, Username and Password to the Council Info Site to anyone.

The Council Info Site contains links to other websites. When you click onto one of those links, you are contacting another website for which the District has no responsibility. For this reason, care is given to ensure that all links provided to other websites are accepted by the District.

Reminder:

  • Do not share the Site Address with anyone.
  • Do not share your Username or Password.

If you forget your password, or if for any reason you believe that the site address; username or password information has been disclosed, please promptly alert the Manager or Deputy Manager of Legislative Services.
