Accessibility in Saanich

Saanich is a vibrant community of neighbourhoods where people of all ages, abilities and cultures chose to live, learn, work and play. The municipality is welcoming and inclusive of all people. We respect individual differences and recognize that they provide value to our community.

Saanich supports a number of programs designed to address barriers to participation:

  • Low and no-cost programming
  • Get Active Program

Low and No-Cost Programs – Best For Less

Look in our Active Living Guide for low cost and free recreation opportunities.

Leisure Involvement For Everyone program (LIFE)

Are you living on a fixed or low income? Visit one of your four Saanich Recreation Centres and ask about LIFE. Our LIFE program is designed to keep individuals and families moving without a price tag attached! Let’s not let numbers on paper stop you from living a healthy active lifestyle!

Looking for a more affordable way to recreate? Participate in free swims and skates sponsored by Coast Capital Savings:

Swim

  • Third Friday of the month at Gordon Head Recreation Centre from 7:00 pm – 8:30 pm

Skate

Last Friday of the month at Pearkes Recreation Centre from 5:45 pm – 6:45 pm

For more information on low and no cost programming contact Mena @ 250-475-5407 or email mena.westhaver@saanich.ca

Get Active

Are you new to the area and facing barriers that may prevent you from participating in our recreation services? We would be pleased have a facilitator give you a tour of the facility you are interested in attending. The facilitator will guide you through the best way to access our services. Contact Jason @ 250-475-5427 or email jason.jones@saanich.ca to arrange a tour.

The municipality of Saanich is commited to ensuring all our facilities are accessible to our residents. All Centres have :

  • Designated disabled parking stall
  • Extra wide parking stalls for ramped accessible vehichles
  • Automatic doors into the building
