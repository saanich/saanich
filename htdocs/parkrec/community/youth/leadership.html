Youth Summer Internship Program

16 - 18 yrs

If you have an interest in gaining work experience this summer, this program could be for you!

The Youth Summer Internship program supports youth aged 16 years + in building employment readiness skills through a month long mentored placement in the Municipality of Saanich.

This program offers the opportunity to work in a day camp setting with Recreation, outdoors with Saanich Parks, behind a computer with the Technology Division or exploring green initiatives in the Planning Department.

Participants who successfully complete the program will receive a $500 honorarium.

Previous leadership training and experience is an asset.

Resume Deadline

June 1, annually.

Saanich Youth Services is committed to be a leader in building developmental assets in young people. One significant way we desire to impact youth is through our leadership continuum. This is an opportunity for a young person to learn valuable leadership skills, be mentored in a recreation environment, gain practical work experience and have the potential to gain employment in our department.
