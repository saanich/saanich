Meet our professional instructors

Craig Rencher - CPGA Head Professional

Craig joined the Canadian Professional Golfers Association in 1980, and has held positions of General Manager at Arbutus Ridge Golf & Country Club in Cobble Hill from 1988 to 2001, Associate Professional at Marine Drive Golf Club in Vancouver from 1981 to 1988, and Assistant Professional at Chilliwack Golf & Country Club in 1980.

Craig coached the Camosun College Men's and Ladies' golf teams from 1995 - 1997, and was an active instructor, facilitator, and course developer of the Professional Golf Management Program at Camosun from 1995 to 2001. He was selected as the British Columbia Professional Golfers Association "Head Professional of the Year" in 1997.

Craig envisions Cedar Hill Golf Course as the area leader in promoting and developing junior golf programs for children as young as age four.

Lee J.H. Lee - CPGA Professional

Lee J.H. Lee has been at Cedar Hill Golf Club since 2006 and became a CPGA professional in 2008. Titleist Performance Institute since 2009. Studied Political Science at University of Calgary 2000-2005. UBC Sauder School of Business 2010.  Lee JH Lee is best known for his work on Combination Effect where he contends that every element in a golf swing has to be negated or combined with counter action, or counter non-action, on the opposing movement group.  C-Effect has proven to be successful in providing immediate solution to people with mobility or flexibility issues where compensation is necessary for excessive or lack of movements.  Lee's philosophy is to provide immediate solution to better ball striking while necessitating a long term fitness goal that can better distance.  For more information, please visit:
