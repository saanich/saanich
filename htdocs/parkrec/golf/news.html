Golf Course Public Consultation

September 20, 2012

In response to the outcomes from the Elevate and Chemistry consultations that took place in the spring and early summer, the existing licenced light snack and beverage service will be relocated to the upstairs area at the golf course club house. This will take place towards the end of this year or early in 2013 following completion of the deck restoration work at the club house. Banquet service to golf and outside events will continue and promotion of this service will be a focus.

In using the upstairs space for the current food service operation, the common message that was heard through the consultations is being endorsed and the golf course patrons, trail users and general public will be able to enjoy the one of a kind setting and view.

The reports from Elevate Consulting and Chemistry Consulting Group Inc. can be viewed here:

  • Elevate Consulting:
    • Stakeholder Engagement & Public Consultation on the Future of the Cedar Hill Golf Course Clubhouse - Facility Use and Promotional Options ( PDF)
  • Chemistry Consulting Group Inc.:
    • Review of Foodservice Operations At Cedar Hill Golf Course ( PDF)

If you would like more information or have questions please contact Saanich Parks and Recreation (recreation@saanich.ca, 250 475-5422).

Frost and Golf

Please be reminded of the approved frost policy. Early start times that are affected by frost are cancelled, contact the pro shop and the staff will try and re-book these affected times.

Golfer’s Hint

Watch the forecast, if you see clear skies and a night time low of less than 7 degrees (C), you may want to cancel and then re-book. You can also phone the pro-shop, however this is a slower alternative.
