Cedar Hill Golf Course is a 5,200 yard, par 67 course, located 10 minutes from downtown Victoria and open year round for public play.

We are a full facility, 18 hole golf course offering CPGA lessons and affordable green fee golf. We also host tournaments. For non-golfers, the course is surrounded by a picturesque 3.5 km (2.2 mile) chip trail. Whether you're a walker, runner or nature enthusiast, you won't want to miss the wonderful views this wooded trail offers.

The Clubhouse includes a licensed light food service for golfers and the general public together with tasty buffet choices for golf and non-golf events alike. For further information call 250-475-7150.

The ideal year round weather in Victoria, makes for a great golf experience when combined with Green fees that are an excellent value for the quality of play offered.

The fully stocked pro shop has all the golf items you will require. There is a large fleet of power and pull carts, as well as club rentals available for you at extremely low prices.

Feel free to drop by our Short Game Practice and Learning Center. We offer several instructional programs for all levels of play, as well as an ideal short game facility for you to work on your game. Range balls are rented by the bucket at unbelievably low prices.
