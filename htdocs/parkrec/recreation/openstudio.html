Arts and Ceramics Open Studio Drop-in Programs

Whether you’re a beginner practicing your technique, or an established artist developing your body of work, you’ll find inspiration in our new purpose-built studio facilities and welcoming shared studio
environment.

Through registering in either the Art Open Studio Drop-in or Ceramics Open Studio Drop-in program, you will enjoy many hours of weekly access to state-of-the-art studio spaces, tools and equipment during the available studio drop-in times.

Art Open Studio Drop-in Program

Find creative inspiration in our new and welcoming art studios during available drop-in times. Includes easels and available equipment, including an environmental "SmartWasher" for oil paints, as well as a small storage space for your supplies and works in progress. Basic skills are required to use the studio independently. Must be 16 years or older to register; spaces are limited.
Phone 475-7121 or drop-in to Cedar Hill to register.

Orientation Required:

You must complete an art studio orientation to gain access to the studio. This orientation provides important information on using the studio and the equipment provided. You may book your orientation at the time of registration.

Cost:

Three month open studio access is available based on the following sessions and fees.

October 1 - December 31, 2013* $100 plus GST
January 1 - March 31, 2014 * $100 plus GST

* Pro-rated if you register part way through a session.

Drop-in Schedules:

You must be registered in the Art Open Studio Drop-in program to use the facility during drop-in times.

Art Studio Fall Drop-in Schedule ~ October 1 - December 31, 2013

Art Studio Features:

  • easels
  • cutting table
  • drying racks
  • taborets
  • spot lighting
  • environmental sink disposal system

Ceramics Open Studio Drop-in Program

Enjoy our new ceramics studio during available drop-in times. Includes use of wheels, tools, and equipment as well as a small storage space for your clay and supplies. Two previous pottery classes are required to use the studio independently. Must be 16 years or older to register; spaces are limited.
Phone 475-7121 or drop-in to Cedar Hill to register.

Orientation Required:

You must complete a Ceramics Studio Orientation to gain access to the studio. This orientation provides important information on using the studio and the equipment provided. You may book your orientation at the time of registration.

Cost:

Three month open studio access is available based on the following sessions and fees.

October 1 - December 31, 2013 $125 plus GST
January 1 - March 31, 2014 $150 plus GST

* Pro-rated if you register part way through a session.

Drop-in Schedules:

You must be registered in the Ceramics Open Studio Drop-in program or have purchased a Pottery Access Card as part of your ceramics class to use the facility during drop-in times.

Ceramic Studio Fall Drop-in Schedule ~ October 1 - December 31, 2013

Clay:

For safety and equipment protection, clay used in the Ceramics studio must be purchased through the Arts Centre. Cost is $35 plus GST per 22lb bag of standard M340 stoneware clay, or $10 plus GST per bag of recycled clay. Cost includes glazes and firings. Ask the Ceramics Technician about specialty clays.
