Flo Bongiovanni-Russell, Bsc PE, MA

Everfitt Movement rehabilitation sessions guide clients toward a healthy lifestyle. Everfitt Staff teach clients to respect the mind/body connection and focus on self improvement. Sessions take place in the Cedar Hill Recreation Centre weight room and are fun, motivating as well as tailored to individual needs. Using a sound knowledge base of assessment tools and exercise prescription, Everfitt Staff develop confidence within the client. Everfitt Staff are qualified and certified Practicing Kinesiologists.

Rehabilitation sessions are suitable for clients with back and joint pain, chronic illnesses, post-surgery conditioning, motor vehicle accidents or work related injuries. New clients can expect to start with a consultation that includes a health history assessment used to evaluate the client’s current physical condition, followed by a discussion about possible health outcomes and goals. Following the consultation, clients can then work with the Kinesiologist to finalize personal goals and choose the service that assists them in reaching those goals.

Aquatic Exercise Therapy

Exercise therapy in the Pool setting can be extremely beneficial for those recovering from injury or dealing with chronic conditions.

The natural buoyancy of the water takes compression off your joints and adds a natural resistance supporting your body while strengthening. The hydrostatic pressure of the water can decrease inflammation making Aquatherapy an excellent choice for those with arthritis, low back pain, hip and knee injuries, and post-surgical recovery.

Shallow Water Aquatherapy

This class focuses on gentle strength and mobility exercises in the shallow water for individuals with stable medical conditions and those recovering from injuries or illnesses. The focus of this class is on posture, balance and core stability while working both the upper and lower body against the gentle resistance of the water. This group-led class requires and initial assessment and introduction to water exercises to ensure each participant's need and limitations are addressed during the classes. This class is great for anyone who needs to improve their balance and overall conditioning in a semi-buoyant environment.

Mon/Wed/Fri 10am

 

Deep Water Aquatherapy

This class focuses on cardiovascular conditioning, strength, mobility, posture and core stability exercises in the deep water using flotations devices. A portion of the class is also spent in the shallow water working on gait, balance and posture. This group-led class requires an initial assessment and introduction to water exercises to ensure participant's needs and limitations are addressed during the classes. This class is ideal for individuals who are very comfortable in the water and who are looking for a slightly higher intensity of workout while focusing on core strength and stability and performing stafe, specific and effective strength and mobility exercises for the upper and lower body. The fun, social environment will keep you coming back for more!

Mon/Wed Fri 11am

Massage Therapy

Registered Massage Therapists (RMTs) in B.C. are health care professionals trained in the assessment and diagnosis of the soft tissues and joints of the body. Research supports the use of massage therapy as an effective approach in injury management and rehabilitation, as well as part of a preventive care program.

British Columbians can be sure that they are receiving the highest quality of care available because RMTs in BC are governed by the College of Massage Therapists of British Columbia (CMTBC), the profession's equivalent to medicine's College of Physicians and Surgeons. Effective for migraine headaches, tendonitis, arthritis, sports injuries and many other conditions/injuries as well as for general relaxation.
