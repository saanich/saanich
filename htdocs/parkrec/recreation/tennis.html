Booking a Court

Courts are available during Facility Hours.

Two day advance booking. One phone in time at 9:30 am. After the designated phone in time, you may book any time still available. Courts may be booked for either 1, 1.5 or 2 hour periods by calling the centre.

Court Cancellation Policy

Please be sure to cancel at least 12 hours prior to playing time. Without proper notification, you will be charged regular court fees which also apply to a No Show.
