Pearkes

Two full sheets of ice offering:

  • Skating & Hockey programs for all ages and skill levels
  • Powerskating programs
  • Private skating lessons
  • Ice rental opportunities available

Public Drop-In sessions include:

  • Everyone Welcome Skates
  • Parent and Tot Ice Play (under 6 years hockey and fun activities)
  • Adult Only Public Skates (19+)
  • Duffer Hockey (19+)
  • Adult Figure Skating ( 19+)
  • Stick and Puck

For safety reasons, figure skating and hockey playing are not permitted during public skating (unless specified)

  • Baby strollers are permitted on the ice, as are wheelchairs
  • All patrons must wear skates on the ice

The G.R. Pearkes indoor arenas are home to the Saanich Jr. Braves Hockey Organization, the Saanich Minor Hockey Association, the Saanich Figure Skating Club and Saanich Lacrosse.
