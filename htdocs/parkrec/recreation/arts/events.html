The Arts Centre at Cedar Hill

Most of the gallery and showcase exhibitions offered at the Arts Centre at Cedar Hill are managed through a partnership with the Community Arts Council of Greater Victoria. For information and applications, please visit the Community Arts Council of Greater Victoria.


On occasion, The District of Saanich collaborates with community groups and arts organizations and artists to host community exhibitions at the Arts Centre at Cedar Hill. Contact Arts Centre staff for more information.
