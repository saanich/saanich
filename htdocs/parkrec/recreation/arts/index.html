Arts For Everyone!

At the Arts Centre at Cedar Hill, there is something for everyone to enjoy and participate in. We offer a range of programs for all ages and levels in visual arts, ceramics, dance and performing arts. Visit our regularly changing exhibitions in the galleries and showcases, as well as special arts-based events.

Convenient Location and Inspiring Setting

Enjoy our bright, spacious facility located in a safe, central community setting. Register for a class or a studio drop-in program, or stop in to visit the Centre’s exhibitions, displays and the Gallery Café. Free parking and regular bus service make our facility a great choice for experiencing the arts.
