Discover the Best of Local Arts!

May is the month when many artists around Saanich welcome visitors into their studios for behind the scenes tours of their works and where they create. Studio tours feature well- known local artists and include a diverse range of arts, including painting, glass, ceramics, textiles, jewellery and sculpture.

Tours are staggered throughout the month of May so that you can take part in several different neighbourhood tours or find one that fits your schedule. So whether you are new to visiting a studio or a devoted studio tour regular, you are sure to have a memorable experience and discover something wonderful!

Free of charge and everyone welcome.
