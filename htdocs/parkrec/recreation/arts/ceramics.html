Programs

Our experienced and supportive instructors offer a range of classes and workshops for all ages and levels in hand-built and wheel-based pottery and ceramics. We also offer fun and creative childrens’ spring and summer camps.

More information on upcoming classes and workshops is available under “Arts- Crafts & Visual” in the Adult, Teen, School Age and Early Childhood sections of the Active Living Guide.

Customized programs for your organization or group:

We work with a number of organizations to offer specialized programs. Dependent upon availability, our studio and instructors are available to develop multi-class or one-time programs to suit your needs and interests.
Contact us for more information.

Studio Features

  • 12 electric potters wheels
  • oxidation kilns contained in their own compound
  • a 20 inch Brent slab roller
  • a large work table exclusively for hand building
  • concrete wedging tables with wall mounted cutting wire
  • several stoneware clay bodies & recycled clay
  • ware carts to minimize the handling of your work
  • many colourful cone 6 stoneware glazes, under-glazes, coloured slips and stains
  • stainless steel trough sinks with multiple faucets
  • technical support to answer your questions
