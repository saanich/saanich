Programs

We offer a range of dance and performing arts classes for all ages in a variety of styles including ballet, jazz, tap, hip-hop, line dancing, African dance and creative drum and dance. We also offer popular spring and summer camps in musical theatre, ballet, and acting.

Information on upcoming classes and workshops is available under “Arts- Dance” in the Adult, Teen, School Age and Early Childhood sections of the Active Living Guide.
