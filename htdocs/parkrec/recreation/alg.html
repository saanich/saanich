Quick Guide Instructions - about 20 seconds to read...

Click on any page to zoom into the page details  The TABLE OF CONTENTS is hyperlinked and you can navigate to your favourite section right from there! Find the 'search box' & type in a keyword  to do a search by topic. Other things:

  1. ALL of the lessons and programs CAN BE REGISTERED INTO FROM THE GUIDE by clicking upon the SIX DIGIT COURSE CODE found near each course description. You will see that the COURSE CODE CHANGES COLOUR when you move your mouse pointer over the code.
  2. 'Click' on the video windows to start or stop a video.
  3. All of our Guides are viewable and fully functional on mobile devices including smartphones (with the exception of some models of blackberry devices )
  4. To print off a page please DO NOT use the drop down print function on your browser. Simply click on the printer icon found on the lower right hand corner of the zmag viewing window (once you open the publication).
