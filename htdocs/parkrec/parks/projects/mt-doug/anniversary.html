20th Anniversary Project

The 20th anniversary will be recognized with a special event on November 22, 2012 with local politicians, staff, the Friends of Mt Doug and general public in attendance.

The original park charter will be prominently featured on a large rock placed at the base of Churchill Drive. This rock will be the first phase of a larger installation to be developed in the near future that includes additional "character" rocks, an information kiosk, feature paving and a new decorative gate treatment. These additions will add to the special sense of arrival one experiences when entering the park whether it be by vehicle, bike or walking.
