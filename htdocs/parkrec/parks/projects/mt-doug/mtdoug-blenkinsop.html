A new parking lot and trail has been completed on the Blenkinsop Road side of Mount Douglas Park.  This rugged trail passes through Garry oak and Douglas fir forests, and features sweeping views of the Blenkinsop Valley.  At the top end, it connects with the Tod Trail.  At the bottom end, a new cross-walk connects the Blenkinsop Trail with Lohbrunner Rd. and the Lochside Regional Trail.  Click here for a copy of the concept plan.

The parking lot used open graded asphalt to encourage stormwater infiltration on-site.  Also, a botanical consultant was engaged to guide the trail development.  Although the terrain and property boundaries limited the options for trail routing, the trail was designed to minimize ecological impacts and erosion wherever possible.

Like the rest of Mount Douglas Park, we ask that people respect the natural environment by staying on designated trails. Contact Saanich Parks at parks@saancih.ca or 250-475-5522 if you have any questions or comments on this project.
