Saanich Parks and Recreation is pleased to announce that Phase 2 of the Harvest Lane Park concept plan is now complete.  The federal Recreation Infrastructure Canada (RInC) program allowed us to complete 10 playground projects in 2010 allowing us to expedite many of the projects we had in our long range plan.

Phase 1 (2008) of the project involved re-grading the entire park, installation of the trail portion of the San Juan Greenway and some tree planting. Phase 2 completes the park with the installation of a small playground, park furniture, a map sign and additional tree planting.

If you have any questions regarding the project, please contact Mike Goldsworthy, Park Planner/Designer, Saanich Parks at 475-5522.
