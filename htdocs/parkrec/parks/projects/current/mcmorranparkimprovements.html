They say good things come in small packages and McMorran Park at just 0.08 hectares (8,870 square feet) certainly falls into that category. Be sure to check out the view, the beach and the improvements we're making to this spectacular little park. 

Saanich acquired the small waterfront park in 2010 from the McMorran family - former owners of the adjacent restaurant. In the past the McMorran family maintained this small patch of lawn for community use. Saanich wanted to continue this practice and have begun to make some minor improvements to the park in order for it to remain a community asset.

A small plaza complete with an interpretive sign (make the words interpretive sign a link to the sign) that tells the McMorran family story and has a map of Saanich Parks and Trails on the other side. The sidewalks fronting the park have been improved, invasive English ivy has been removed and an irrigation system and some hedging plants have also been installed.
