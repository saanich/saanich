Beckwith Water Park

Located on Beckwith Avenue just off Quadra Street the "Frog Pond" at Beckwith Park has all kinds of water spray features to help keep you cool. With an adjacent playground, lawn area, trails, picnic tables, and washrooms this park has all you need for great summer fun.

Water Park hours of operation:
July 1st - Labour Day 10:00 am - 6:00 pm
June and September noon to 4:00 pm (weather permitting)

Beach Fires

As of November 1, 2010 beach fires are no longer permitted in Saanich.

For more information contact Fire Prevention, 760 Vernon Avenue, (250) 475-5500

Park and Sport Field Permit Procedures and Guidelines

The Park and Sport Field Permit Procedures and Guidelines serves as a resource to assist in the management of activities in public parks.  The guidelines and regulations are in place to ensure enjoyable park use, conserve park spaces, protect public safety and provide a planning resource for organizing successful activities.

 

 

Park Washrooms

There are 4 Parks with washrooms open year-round:

Cadboro-Gyro Park
Mt Douglas Park
Craigflower Park
Beckwith Park

3 Parks have washrooms that are open seasonally (May 1 to October 30)

Playfair Park
Cordova Bay Park
Majestice Park

Other parks have washrroms that are maintained by Sports Clubs and are not currently designed for public access. However, some of these facilities can be made available by park use permit for picnics and family events.
