Beckwith Water Park Closure

Beckwith Water Park - Closed for Repairs – Wednesday, August 14, 2013

A break in a water mainline to the park will be repaired on August 14. As a result, the washrooms and water park will be closed for 1 day. The playground and remainder of the Park remains open, and there will be porta-potties on location. We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience to our summer water-park customers! If you have any questions, please contact parks@saanich.ca or (250) 475-5522.
