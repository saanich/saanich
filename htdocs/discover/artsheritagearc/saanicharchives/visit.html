Location & Hours

Saanich Archives is located inside the Saanich Centennial Library, next to Pearkes Recreation Centre, at 3100 Tillicum Road. Please enter the library and follow signs to the Archives. Saanich Archives is wheelchair accessible. Free parking is available next to Pearkes Recreation Centre.

Open Monday to Friday 10am to 4:30pm (closed statutory holidays)

Driving Directions & Bus Information

Reference Room

  • Visitors are welcome to browse reference materials which include books on B.C. history, pioneer files, copies of photographs and maps, directories, oral history interviews, and changing exhibits
  • Staff are on hand to answer questions and help with your research
  • Finding aids are available for access to the original archival records, which are stored on-site in a climate controlled vault
  • Saanich Archives is fully wheelchair accessible
  • Please note that no food or drink is permitted in the Archives
  • Researchers are welcome to use cameras (some restrictions apply)
