Use the Estimate Checklist to help you decide on the requirements for your project.

Foundation

  • Type, quality of mortar or concrete, colour
  • Drainage - perimeter and storm drains
  • Water/weather sealer
  • Ventilation
  • Skirting details
  • Unforseen rot/repair (ask contractor to indicate on Estimate Sheet the hourly rate for this)

Gutters and Downspouts

  • Material, type, colour, style, gauge
  • Fastening system (ie. nails, screws, spacings)
  • Connection to drainage system
  • Connection seals/covers
  • Repair rot/damage to fascia boards or rafter tails
  • Storm drain condition
  • Gutters adjusted to drain toward downspouts
  • Unforseen rot/repair (ask contractor to indicate on Estimate Sheet the hourly rate for this)

Roof

  • Tear off/preparation (remove all old roofing)
  • Repair damaged strapping or roof sheathing
  • Unforseen rot/repair (ask contractor to indicate on Estimate Sheet the hourly rate for this)
  • New roof material
  • Shingles - type, grade, untreated or preservative treated
  • Ridge, capping type, other (description)
  • Valleys and flashing (material, colour, gauge)
  • Rafter tails, eaves, barges, soffits (repair replace material, type)
  • Placement of vents (as per Building Code) number, colour, material
  • Eave - check nail lengths to avoid protruding under eave
  • Warranty - minimum 10 years

Chimneys and Masonry

  • Type, style, colour
  • Mortar - type, style, colour
  • Ties, strapping, bracings, reinforcement
  • Water/weather sealer

Re-Chimneys

  • Type, liners, protrusion
  • Finish capping, type, style, sealer, chimney pots
  • Bricks to match original
  • Insulation - air space (if applicable)
  • Unforseen rot/repair (ask contractor to indicate on Estimate Sheet the hourly rate for this)

Exterior Woodwork Trim

  • Wood quality, type, kiln dried, profile match, mouldings
  • Fastenings (ie.) glue nails, screws
  • Back priming wood before installation
  • Unforseen rot/repair (ask contractor to indicate on Estimate Sheet the hourly rate for this)
  • Preparation and finish

Painting

  • Detailed surface preparation (ie.) priming, cleaning process, repairs
  • Paint brand, type - oil, latex
  • Number of coats
  • Warranty
