The District of Saanich completed a re-development and expansion of the Gordon Head Recreation Centre in February 2010. Upgrades were made to the weight room and change rooms and an addition of a dance studio and multipurpose. The renovation included energy efficiency improvements with the installation of a green roof and a solar water heating system. Another component of this initiative was the selection and installation of a work of public art. A Public Art Competition was held and Fran Baskerville’s artwork was the winning submission, chosen through a jury process. The Jury comprised of three local artists, a representative from the local neighbourhood association, and a Saanich Parks & Recreation staff member. The chosen work of art addresses the broad theme of “Multi-culturalism & Community”.

Fran Baskerville’s art work, “In the Moment”, is a glimpse of Gordon Head; a community of people sharing a landscape and home where distinct ethnic backgrounds, ages, abilities and activity choices are valued and supported.

The original artwork was digitally scanned for printing on to a 10% open window covering fabric by SunGlow Window Coverings. The art work on the window coverings provides a colourful sunshade. The shade material allows some light penetration that further illuminates the artwork. Installation of the work was complete in August 2010.
