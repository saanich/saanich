Saanich is a municipality within the Greater Victoria Area, located on Vancouver Island.   We've provided some links here for getting around on the bus, as well as getting to the island itself.

BC TRANSIT
Everything you need to know about using the Victoria Regional Transit System is on this site. Transit service operates seven days a week, 365 days a year.

BC FERRIES
Whether you are getting on or off Vancouver Island or travelling to one of the smaller islands , the BC Ferry web site will give you schedules, fares and current conditions.

 
VICTORIA AIRPORT
Victoria International Airport is located 22 km north of Victoria.  Visit their web site for information on air travel in and out of Victoria.
CYCLING
There are many commuter and scenic bike paths throughout Saanich.
